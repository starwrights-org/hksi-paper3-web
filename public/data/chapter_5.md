## 第1题 (ID: 155637)

### 中文题目

在评估是否批准超逾持仓上限的申请时，证监会必须审慎考虑若干因素。判断以下哪些是证监会必须考虑的因素？
I. 超逾持仓上限是否会损害投资大众的利益。
II. 授权是否与《证券及期货条例》的法定规管目标一致。
III. 相关期货或股票期权合约的市场流通量。
IV. 申请人是否已连续三年盈利。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

In assessing whether to approve an application to exceed the prescribed position limit,the SFC must carefully consider several factors. Determine which of the following are factors the SFC must consider?
I. Whether exceeding the position limit would be detrimental to the interests of the investing public.
II. Whether the authorization would be consistent with the statutory regulatory objectives of the Securities and Futures Ordinance.
III. The market liquidity of the relevant futures or stock options contracts.
IV. Whether the applicant has been profitable for three consecutive years.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：A**


### 答案解析

I 正确。证监会须信纳超逾规限不会损害投资大众的利益。
II 正确。证监会须考虑授权会否与其法定规管目标不一致。
III 正确。证监会须顾及相关合约的流通量。
IV 错误。连续三年盈利并非相关规定的明确要求。


### Explanation

I. Correct. The SFC must be satisfied that exceeding the limit would not be detrimental to the interests of the investing public.
II. Correct. The SFC must consider whether the authorization would be inconsistent with its statutory regulatory objectives.
III. Correct. The SFC must have regard to the liquidity of the relevant contracts.
IV. Incorrect. Three consecutive years of profitability is not an explicit requirement under the relevant provisions.

## 第2题 (ID: 155640)

### 中文题目

B公司是在联交所上市的一种衍生权证的发行人。该公司为对冲其因提供该权证流通量而产生的风险，需要持有相关的股票期权合约，但此对冲持仓可能超过规定的上限。根据规则，B公司可以如何合法地持有此超限仓位？
A. B公司无法获得授权，因为持仓上限规则不适用于上市结构性产品的发行人。
B. B公司需要向香港联合交易所有限公司申请授权，因为其超限持仓是为了对冲先前就流通量供应活动而产生的风险。
C. B公司可以直接建立超限仓位，因为所有上市产品发行人的对冲活动都天然豁免于持仓上限。
D. B公司必须将其对冲需求分解至多个关联公司，使每个公司的持仓都不超限。


### English

Company B is an issuer of derivative warrants listed on the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong. To hedge the risks arising from its provision of liquidity for these warrants,the company needs to hold relevant stock option positions,but these hedging positions may exceed the prescribed limits. According to the rules,how can Company B legally hold these excess positions?
A. Company B cannot obtain authorization,as position limit rules do not apply to issuers of listed structured products.
B. Company B needs to apply to the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited for authorization,as its excess positions are for hedging risks arising from previous liquidity provision activities.
C. Company B may directly establish positions exceeding the limit,as all hedging activities by issuers of listed products are naturally exempt from position limits.
D. Company B must break down its hedging needs among multiple associated companies so that each company&#039;s positions do not exceed the limit.


**正确答案：B**


### 答案解析

A错误。规则明确列出了在联交所上市的结构性产品的若干发行人（及其有连系公司）在符合特定条件（为对冲流通量供应活动产生的风险）时，可以被授权超逾持仓上限。
B正确。联交所可授权在联交所上市的结构性产品的若干发行人（及该等发行人的若干有连系公司）超逾持仓上限，前提是其持有相关合约是为了对冲先前就进行流通量供应活动而取得的持仓所产生的风险。
C错误。发行人需要获得联交所的明确授权才能超逾上限，并非天然豁免。
D错误。规则允许在获得授权的情况下持有超限仓位以满足对冲需求，采取分解至多个公司的做法可能涉及控制关系的合并计算，并非必要或推荐的合规路径。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The rules explicitly specify that certain issuers (and their connected companies) of structured products listed on the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong may be authorized to exceed position limits,subject to meeting specific conditions (for hedging risks arising from liquidity provision activities).
B is correct. The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong may authorize certain issuers of structured products listed on it (and certain connected companies of such issuers) to exceed position limits,provided they hold the relevant contracts to hedge risks arising from positions previously acquired for carrying out liquidity provision activities.
C is incorrect. Issuers need explicit authorization from the Stock Exchange to exceed the limit; exemption is not automatic.
D is incorrect. The rules allow holding excess positions for hedging needs when authorized. Splitting positions among multiple companies may involve aggregation rules regarding control relationships and is not a necessary or recommended compliance path.


### 温习册出处

由香港期货交易所有限公司或香港联合交易所有限公司授权超逾订明上限
期交所或联交所可授权以下人士超逾《合约持仓量及申报规则》订明的持仓上限：
(a) 获期交所／联交所注册为庄家或流通量供应者（视属何情况而定）的人士；
(b) 在联交所上市的结构性产品的若干发行人（及该等发行人的若干有连系公司）持有或控制相关期货或股票期权合约是为了对冲先前就进行流通量供应活动而取得该等上市结构性产品的持仓量所产生的风险；及
(c) 该人士属于香港期货结算有限公司或香港联合交易所期权结算所有限公司的结算所参与者，而该参与者为上文(a)或(b)所述已获授权超逾持仓上限的人士所持有的相关期货或股票期权合约进行结算。

## 第3题 (ID: 155644)

### 中文题目

一家量化对冲基金主要交易恒指波幅指数期货以管理投资组合的波动性风险。基金经理想了解该合约的持仓限制规定。根据规则，以下说法正确的是？
A. 该基金在任何一个合约月的持仓超过500份未平仓合约即须申报，且在任何单一合约月的持仓上限为10000份。
B. 该基金在任何一个合约月的持仓超过1000份未平仓合约即须申报，但其所有合约月合计的持仓没有上限。
C. 该基金在任何一个合约月的持仓超过1000份未平仓合约即须申报，且在任何单一合约月的持仓上限为10000份未平仓合约。
D. 该基金的持仓上限是根据其净仓位（所有合约月合计）计算的，上限为10000份好仓或淡仓净额。


### English

A quantitative hedge fund primarily trades Hang Seng Volatility Index futures to manage the volatility risk of its investment portfolio. The fund manager wants to understand the position limit regulations for this contract. According to the rules,which of the following statements is correct?
A. The fund&#039;s position exceeding 500 open contracts in any one contract month would require reporting,and the position limit in any single contract month is 10,000 contracts.
B. The fund&#039;s position exceeding 1,000 open contracts in any one contract month would require reporting,but its positions across all contract months combined have no upper limit.
C. The fund&#039;s position exceeding 1,000 open contracts in any one contract month would require reporting,and the position limit in any single contract month is 10,000 open contracts.
D. The fund&#039;s position limit is calculated based on its net position (across all contract months combined),with an upper limit of 10,000 net long or short contracts.


**正确答案：C**


### 答案解析

A错误。恒指波幅指数期货的须申报水平是任何一个合约月1000份未平仓合约，不是500份。
B错误。恒指波幅指数期货存在持仓上限，即任何一个合约月10000份未平仓合约，并非所有合约月合计没有上限。
C正确。对于恒指波幅指数期货合约，须申报水平为任何一个合约月1000份未平仓合约；订明上限（持仓上限）为任何一个合约月10000份未平仓合约。
D错误。恒指波幅指数期货的上限是基于单一合约月的未平仓合约数，而非所有合约月合计的净额。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The reporting level for Hang Seng Volatility Index futures is 1,000 open contracts in any one contract month,not 500.
B is incorrect. Hang Seng Volatility Index futures have a position limit,which is 10,000 open contracts in any one contract month; it is not that positions across all contract months combined have no limit.
C is correct. For Hang Seng Volatility Index futures contracts,the reporting level is 1,000 open contracts in any one contract month; the prescribed limit (position limit) is 10,000 open contracts in any one contract month.
D is incorrect. The limit for Hang Seng Volatility Index futures is based on the number of open contracts in a single contract month,not the net total across all contract months combined.


### 温习册出处

订明上限
除少数例外情况外，所有受申报规定规限的相关期货或股票期权合约也受制于持仓上限，而持仓上限比相应的须申报的水平较高，如以下例子：
(a) 任何人士如持有或控制在联交所上市的股份的股票期货合约，即须受须申报水平所规限（任何一个合约月 1,000 份未平仓合约），而订明上限为25,000 份好仓或淡仓净额合约（所有合约月合计）；
(b) 任何人士如持有或控制恒指波幅指数期货合约，即须受须申报水平所规限（任何一个合约月 1,000 份未平仓合约），而订明上限为任何一个合约月10,000 份未平仓合约；及
(c) 任何人士如持有或控制恒生指数的股票指数期货合约，即须受须申报水平所规限（任何一个合约期 500 份未平仓合约乘以每个指数点 50 港元与该股票指数期货合约相关的合约乘数（见下文附注）的比例），而订明上限为对冲指定资产价格转变风险的限额（10,000 份好仓或淡仓净额，以所有合约期合计）。
附注：合约乘数是某指数（或如为股票指数期货期权合约，则相关指数）的一个点的现金值，或在其他情况下由期交所于合约规定中指明的数值。
任何人士如持有超逾载于《合约持仓量及申报规则》内订明的持仓上限，即属犯罪，惟若他们获授权超逾相关上限则作别论。此类授权可由期交所、联交所或证监会发出。

## 第4题 (ID: 155647)

### 中文题目

钱女士是一家投资公司的交易主管，她控制的一个账户持有的股票期权持仓超过了须申报水平。她按照公司流程，书面授权其助理孙先生每日负责向联交所提交持仓报告。某日，孙先生因疏忽未能提交报告。关于此次未申报的责任，以下哪项说法是正确的？
A. 孙先生作为被委任的代理人，应承担未申报的全部责任。
B. 钱女士作为授权人，其责任已因委派行为而转移，因此不承担责任。
C. 申报责任不可转授，钱女士作为持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士，仍需承担未履行申报责任的最终责任。
D. 责任应由钱女士和孙先生共同承担，因为一个是控制人，一个是实际操作人。


### English

Ms. Qian is the head trader at an investment company. A stock options position held in an account she controls has exceeded the reportable level. According to company policy,she authorized her assistant,Mr. Sun,in writing to be responsible for submitting the position report to the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong each day. One day,Mr. Sun neglected to submit the report due to negligence. Regarding the responsibility for this failure to report,which of the following statements is correct?
A. Mr. Sun,as the appointed agent,should bear full responsibility for the failure to report.
B. Ms. Qian,as the authorizing person,has transferred her responsibility through delegation and therefore bears no liability.
C. The reporting obligation is non-delegable. Ms. Qian,as the person holding or controlling the reportable position,still bears the ultimate responsibility for the failure to fulfill the reporting obligation.
D. Responsibility should be shared jointly by Ms. Qian and Mr. Sun,as one is the controller and the other is the actual operator.


**正确答案：C**


### 答案解析

A错误。代理人可能因其行为承担相应责任，但规则的焦点在于，申报的首要及最终责任仍由持有或控制持仓的人士承担。
B错误。持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士可以委任代理人，但申报责任本身不可转授，该人士仍需承担最终责任。
C正确。即使委任了代理人，由于申报责任不可转授，持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士仍须承担履行申报责任的最终责任。
D错误。虽然两者可能都有责任，但规则明确了责任的不可转授性，强调了控制人的最终责任，而非简单的共同责任。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. While an agent may bear corresponding responsibility for their actions,the focus of the rules is that the primary and ultimate responsibility for reporting still lies with the person holding or controlling the position.
B is incorrect. A person holding or controlling a reportable position may appoint an agent,but the reporting obligation itself is non-delegable,and that person still bears the ultimate responsibility.
C is correct. Even if an agent is appointed,because the reporting obligation is non-delegable,the person holding or controlling the reportable position still bears the ultimate responsibility for fulfilling the reporting obligation.
D is incorrect. While both parties may bear some responsibility,the rules clarify the non-delegable nature of the obligation,emphasizing the ultimate responsibility of the controlling person,rather than simple joint liability.


### 温习册出处

须申报的持仓量
持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士负有向期交所或联交所（按照相关期货或股票期权合约进行买卖的交易所）作出申报的首要责任。相关人士须于开始持有须申报的持仓量当日后的一个营业日内作出书面申报，如该名人士继续持有须申报的持仓量，则即使持仓量维持不变，其仍须于其后每日作出书面申报；只有当持仓量减至低于须申报的水平，申报责任才告终止。
持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士亦可委任代理人代其作出申报，然而，由于申报责任不可转授，持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士仍须承担履行申报责任的最终责任。
有关申报必须载有在每一个有关合约期或期权系列所持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约的数目详情，包括每名其他人士的身份以及所持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约的数目。若须申报的持仓量乃就基金安排而产生，则有关申报必须就须申报的持仓量列明基金（及任何子基金）的名称，以及在每一个有关合约期或期权系列就该基金（或子基金）而持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约数目。

### 中文题目

A. 仅由客户张女士持有，因为她是资产的法定拥有人。
B. 仅由投资顾问陈先生控制，因为他拥有全权委托的交易权。

赵先生是某私募基金的负责人，该基金在周一下午收盘时，其持有的某恒生指数期货合约的未平仓总量首次超过了该合约的须申报水平。根据相关规定，赵先生及其基金应当如何履行申报义务？
A. 赵先生必须在周三（即超过水平后的第二个营业日）前，委托其经纪商向期交所作出首次书面申报。
B. 赵先生必须在周二（即开始持有须申报持仓量的下一个营业日）前，亲自或委任代理人向期交所作出首次书面申报。若持仓保持不变，周三无需再次申报。
C. 赵先生必须在周二（即开始持有须申报持仓量的下一个营业日）前，亲自或委任代理人向期交所作出首次书面申报。若周二收盘时持仓量仍高于须申报水平，则周三必须再次作出书面申报。
D. 申报责任主要由执行交易的经纪商承担，赵先生只需确保经纪商知晓该持仓情况。


### English

Mr. Zhao is the head of a private fund. At the market close on Monday afternoon,the total open position in a particular Hang Seng Index futures contract held by the fund exceeded the reportable level for that contract for the first time. According to relevant regulations,how should Mr. Zhao and his fund fulfill their reporting obligations?
A. Mr. Zhao must entrust his broker to make the first written declaration to the HKFE by Wednesday (i.e.,the second business day after exceeding the level).
B. Mr. Zhao must,in person or through an authorized agent,make the first written declaration to the HKFE by Tuesday (i.e.,the next business day after first holding a reportable position). If the position remains unchanged,no further declaration is required on Wednesday.
C. Mr. Zhao must,in person or through an authorized agent,make the first written declaration to the HKFE by Tuesday (i.e.,the next business day after first holding a reportable position). If the position size at the close of trading on Tuesday still exceeds the reportable level,a further written declaration must be made on Wednesday.
D. The reporting obligation primarily lies with the executing broker,and Mr. Zhao only needs to ensure that the broker is aware of the position.


**正确答案：C**


### 答案解析

A错误。首次书面申报的时限是开始持有须申报持仓量日后的一个营业日内，而非两个营业日。
B错误。即使持仓量维持不变，只要该人士继续持有须申报的持仓量，就必须于首次申报后的每个营业日持续作出书面申报。
C正确。申报须于开始持有须申报持仓量当日后的一个营业日内作出。若继续持有须申报的持仓量，则须于其后每日作出书面申报，无论持仓量是否变化。
D错误。持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士负有向交易所作出申报的首要责任和最终责任，该责任不可转授。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The deadline for the initial written declaration is within one business day after the day on which a person first holds a reportable position,not two business days.
B is incorrect. Even if the position size remains unchanged,as long as the person continues to hold a reportable position,a written declaration must be made on each subsequent business day after the initial declaration.
C is correct. The declaration must be made within one business day after the day on which a person first holds a reportable position. If the person continues to hold a reportable position,a written declaration must be made on each subsequent day,regardless of whether the position size changes.
D is incorrect. The person holding or controlling the reportable position has the primary and ultimate responsibility to make the declaration to the exchange; this responsibility cannot be delegated.


### 温习册出处

须申报的持仓量
持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士负有向期交所或联交所（按照相关期货或股票期权合约进行买卖的交易所）作出申报的首要责任。相关人士须于开始持有须申报的持仓量当日后的一个营业日内作出书面申报，如该名人士继续持有须申报的持仓量，则即使持仓量维持不变，其仍须于其后每日作出书面申报；只有当持仓量减至低于须申报的水平，申报责任才告终止。
持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士亦可委任代理人代其作出申报，然而，由于申报责任不可转授，持有或控制须申报持仓量的人士仍须承担履行申报责任的最终责任。
有关申报必须载有在每一个有关合约期或期权系列所持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约的数目详情，包括每名其他人士的身份以及所持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约的数目。若须申报的持仓量乃就基金安排而产生，则有关申报必须就须申报的持仓量列明基金（及任何子基金）的名称，以及在每一个有关合约期或期权系列就该基金（或子基金）而持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约数目。

## 第32题 (ID: 190118)

### 中文题目

一家投资集团的资产管理子公司，在期交所独立运作并交易恒生指数期货。该子公司的投资决策完全由其自身的基金经理做出，集团总部仅负责其整体财务审计和风险资本要求，不干涉具体交易。根据《合约持仓量及申报规则》，在确定该子公司的期货合约持仓是否触及法定上限时，应如何处理其与集团总部的持仓关系？
A. 必须与集团总部及其他关联公司的所有同类别期货合约持仓合并计算。
B. 只需要与集团总部直接控制的账户持仓合并计算。
C. 由于其投资决策独立，子公司的持仓应单独计算，无需与集团合并。
D. 只有子公司持仓超过须申报水平后，才需要与集团持仓合并。


### English

An investment management subsidiary of an investment group operates independently on the HKFE and trades Hang Seng Index futures. The subsidiary&#039;s investment decisions are made solely by its own fund managers,while the group head office is only responsible for its overall financial audit and risk capital requirements and does not interfere with specific transactions. Under the Contract Holdings and Reporting Rules,when determining whether the subsidiary&#039;s futures contract holdings have reached the statutory limit,how should the holding relationship between it and the group head office be handled?
A. They must be aggregated and calculated together with all holdings of the same class of futures contracts held by the group head office and other associated companies.
B. They only need to be aggregated and calculated with the holdings of accounts directly controlled by the group head office.
C. Since its investment decisions are independent,the subsidiary&#039;s holdings should be calculated separately and do not need to be aggregated with the group&#039;s holdings.
D. Only after the subsidiary&#039;s holdings exceed the reporting level do they need to be aggregated with the group&#039;s holdings.


**正确答案：C**


### 答案解析

A错误。仅因企业架构（如集团与子公司的股权关系）而存在的控制权，若母公司不发出日常交易指示，则不被视为控制了子公司的持仓。
B错误。“控制”的定义关键在于能否独立行使买卖的酌情权，而非简单的股权或资本关系。本题中集团无日常指示，故不构成控制。
C正确。规则中“控制”指能独立于持仓拥有人行使买卖酌情权。由于子公司独立运作，集团无日常控制，因此子公司的持仓应单独计算。
D错误。合并计算的规则适用于为确定申报义务或持仓上限是否被触发时，并非只在超标后才适用。且本题情景不构成“控制”，因此无需合并。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. Control existing solely due to corporate structure (such as a shareholding relationship between a group and its subsidiary) does not constitute control of the subsidiary&#039;s holdings if the parent company does not issue daily trading instructions.
B is incorrect. The key to defining "control" is whether one can independently exercise investment discretion over trading,not a simple equity or capital relationship. In this scenario,since the group does not provide daily instructions,it does not constitute control.
C is correct. Under the rules,"control" refers to the ability to exercise investment discretion over trading independently of the holder of the positions. As the subsidiary operates independently and the group has no day-to-day control,the subsidiary&#039;s holdings should be calculated separately.
D is incorrect. The rules regarding aggregation apply to determining whether reporting obligations or statutory holding limits are triggered,and are not applicable only after the limit has been exceeded. Moreover,since the situation in this scenario does not constitute "control," aggregation is not required.


### 温习册出处

当持仓量是为另一人持有或控制
有关须申报的持仓量和持仓上限的规则适用于由任何人士持有或控制的所有相关持仓量，意思即所有属《合约持仓量及申报规则》规定为同一类别的相关期货或股票期权合约的持仓量须合并计算，以确立某申报或持仓上限是否适用。就此而言，如任何人士可独立于主事人（即持仓量的拥有人）而行使酌情权买卖或出售期货或股票期权持仓量，则该名人士将被视为控制该等相关期货或股票期权合约。
另一方面，如只是因企业架构（例如母公司－附属公司关系）而获得控制权，而并无发出日常指示，母公司将不会被视为控制其附属公司或联属公司所拥有的任何相关期货或股票期权合约。

### 中文题目

D. 该制度只规管那些已经获得证监会发牌的专业投资者，个人投资者不受限制。


### English

Mr. Wang is a fund manager at an asset management company in Hong Kong. The fund he manages buys and sells Hang Seng Index futures contracts through the facilities of the HKFE. When assessing the risks of his positions,he needs to understand the regulatory framework applicable to such contracts. Under Hong Kong&#039;s relevant system,which of the following descriptions regarding the system for preventing the risk of excessive market concentration is correct?
A. This system only applies to stocks traded on the Hong Kong Exchange and does not apply to futures contracts.
B. The core of this system is to set a statutory upper limit on position holdings and require reporting once a specific level is exceeded.
C. This system relies primarily on a non-binding guidance document for enforcement,and violation of the guidance will result in criminal penalties.
D. The system only regulates professional investors who have already obtained a license from the SFC; individual investors are not subject to restrictions.


**正确答案：B**


### 答案解析

A错误，该制度明确适用于通过认可交易所（如期交所）设施买卖的期货合约。
B正确，该制度通过设定持仓量上限（订明上限）以及规定超过须申报水平须履行通知责任来运作。

D错误，该制度适用于持有相关合约持仓量的任何人士，不论其是否已获发牌或注册。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The system explicitly applies to futures contracts bought and sold through the facilities of a recognized exchange (such as the HKFE).
B is correct. The system operates by setting upper limits on position holdings (prescribed limits) and requiring reporting obligations when positions exceed the designated reporting levels.
C is incorrect. Violation of legally binding rules is a criminal offence,whereas the relevant guidance documents have no legal force and are primarily used to clarify policy intentions.
D is incorrect. The system applies to any person holding positions in the relevant contracts,regardless of whether they are licensed or registered.

背景

附注：受《合约持仓量及申报规则》规限的期货合约和股票期权合约于下文中简称为“相关期货或股票期权合约”。
《合约持仓量及申报规则》的运作主要透过规定下列各项：
(a) 须申报水平，即就每份相关期货或股票期权合约订明的未平仓相关期货或股票期权合约总数；
(b) 须申报的持仓量，即相关期货或股票期权合约的未平仓持仓量总数超逾相关合约的须申报水平并须负上申报责任）；及
(c) 订明上限，即被容许持有或控制的相关期货或股票期权合约总数的上限。
《合约持仓量及申报规则》适用于在相关期货或股票期权合约中持有相关持仓量的任何人士，即无论该名人士是否获证监会发牌或注册。
证监会已颁布《持仓限额及大额未平仓合约的申报规定指引》（“《合约持仓量及申报指引》”），旨在协助市场参与者了解《合约持仓量及申报规则》在法规遵守方面的规定。《合约持仓量及申报指引》讨论订明上限、提供有关就须申报的持仓量发出通知的规定的进一步详情，并讨论个别人士（例如代理人）以及就基金遵守法规的规定。该等指引亦载列了寻求证监会授权超逾订明上限的程序。
《合约持仓量及申报规则》具有法律效力，违反有关规则属刑事罪行，最高可被判处罚款 100,000 港元及／或监禁 2 年。另一方面，《合约持仓量及申报指引》并无法律效力，主要用作通知市场参与者证监会就《合约持仓量及申报规则》所持守的政策意向。

一家持牌法团在周四发现，由于操作疏忽，其周二为某客户执行的一笔期货交易未能在规定时限内向客户提供成交单据。根据《成交单据规则》，该法团应如何处理？

B. 在察觉违规后的一个营业日内，向证监会发出书面通知。

### English

A licensed corporation discovered on Thursday that due to an operational oversight,it failed to provide a contract note within the prescribed time limit to a client for a futures transaction executed on Tuesday. According to the Contract Notes Rules,how should the corporation handle this situation?
A. Immediately issue a replacement document to the client privately,without reporting to the SFC.
B. Within one business day of becoming aware of the breach,issue a written notification to the SFC.
C. Wait until the client complains before handling it.
D. Only record the incident internally within the company,without external reporting.


**正确答案：B**


### 答案解析

A错误，私下补发不能免除其向证监会报告的合规义务。

### Explanation

B is correct. An intermediary or an associated entity of an intermediary must issue a written notification to the SFC within one business day of becoming aware that it has failed to comply with a specified provision of the Contract Notes Rules.
C is incorrect. The reporting obligation is proactive and should not passively wait for a client complaint.
D is incorrect. Internal recording is necessary,but it cannot replace the formal written notification to the SFC.

According to the Contract Notes Rules,what rights do clients have?
A. The right only to check their own account balance.

C. The right only to automatically receive documents when a transaction occurs in their account.

### 中文题目

According to the Record Keeping Rules,what core standard must the accounting and transaction records kept by an intermediary meet?

C. They only need to meet the requirements for internal management review.
