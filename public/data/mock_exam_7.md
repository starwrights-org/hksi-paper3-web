## 第1题 (ID: 190915)

### 中文题目

在计算速动资产时，以下哪类资产的扣减率最有可能是0%？
A. 持有的上市股票，但其所属公司正面临重大财务困境。
B. 应收账款，账龄超过180天且未获任何担保。
C. 存放于持牌法团在认可财务机构开设的结算账户中的现金余额，可随时用于支付。
D. 持牌法团持有的对其关联公司的长期贷款。


### English

When calculating liquid assets, which of the following types of assets is most likely to have a haircut of 0%?
A. Listed stocks held,but the issuing company is facing significant financial difficulties.
B. Accounts receivable that are over 180 days overdue and unsecured.
C. Cash balances held in a clearing account opened by the licensed corporation with an authorized financial institution,available for immediate payment.
D. Long-term loans held by the licensed corporation extended to its associated company.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，上市股票虽然有一定流动性，但其价值会波动，且对于有财务困境的公司股票，监管机构可能设定较高的扣减率。
B错误，账龄较长的应收账款流动性差且回收风险高，通常会适用较高的扣减率。
C正确，可随时动用的现金及近似现金项目（如结算账户中的现金余额）通常以全值100%计算，扣减率为0%。
D错误，长期贷款流动性差，不能迅速变现，通常会适用很高的扣减率（可能接近或达到100%）。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. While listed stocks have some liquidity, their value fluctuates, and for stocks of companies facing financial difficulties, regulators may set a high haircut.
B is incorrect. Aged accounts receivable have poor liquidity and high recovery risk, and would typically be subject to a high haircut.
C is correct. Cash and cash equivalent items that are readily available (such as cash balances in a clearing account) are usually valued at 100%, with a haircut of 0%.
D is incorrect. Long-term loans have poor liquidity and cannot be quickly realized, and would typically be subject to a very high haircut (possibly approaching or reaching 100%).


### 温习册出处

速动资产
在草拟《财政资源规则》时，证监会已根据资产变现以偿债的速度来考虑资产的价值。为安全起见，监管机构会分析持牌人的各类资产在需要紧急变现时的价值，并据此制定适用于这些资产的特定规则。
因此，在计算速动资产的价值时，可随时动用的现金及近似现金项目会以全值100%计算；换言之，计算这些资产的速动价值时须扣减的价值为 0%。
另一方面，持牌人可能拥有如房地产的固定资产，以及商誉和专利等无形资产。这些资产不能迅速转换为现金，而转换所需的时间一般为最长。因此，这类资产的扣减率为 100%，即将其价值减至零。
这两种大相迥异的资产之间还有多种资产类别，而各种资产类别的“扣减”百分率各有不同，由监管机构按经验厘定。

## 第2题 (ID: 190791)

### 中文题目

一位个人客户和其全资拥有的公司在同一家期货公司开设账户进行交易。在评估客户信贷风险时，期货公司应如何更审慎地处理这两个账户？
A. 视为两个完全独立的客户，分别设置风险限额
B. 将两个账户视为一个关联组别，合并计算持仓并设置整体风险限额
C. 只对个人账户设置限额，公司账户无需限制
D. 以公司账户为准，忽略个人账户


### English

An individual client and their wholly-owned company both opened accounts for trading at the same futures firm. How should the futures firm more prudently handle these two accounts when assessing client credit risk?
A. Treat them as two completely independent clients and set risk limits separately.
B. Treat the two accounts as one connected group,consolidate their positions,and set an overall risk limit.
C. Only set limits for the individual account; the corporate account does not need limits.
D. Focus solely on the corporate account and ignore the individual account.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，当客户之间存在明确的控制关系时，分别管理可能低估了实际的集中风险。
B正确，由同一自然人全资控制的个人与公司账户构成关联客户组别。为有效管理信贷风险，应从整体角度合并评估其持仓，并设置相应的整体风险限额，这有助于更全面地识别和控制集中风险敞口。
C错误，公司账户同样存在信贷风险，且与个人账户的风险可能相互关联。
D错误，忽视任何关联方的持仓都会导致风险监控出现漏洞。


### Explanation

A incorrect, when a clear control relationship exists between clients, separate management may underestimate the actual concentration risk.
B correct, personal and corporate accounts wholly controlled by the same natural person constitute a connected client group. To manage credit risk effectively, their positions should be assessed on a consolidated basis from an overall perspective, with an appropriate overall risk limit set. This approach helps identify and control concentrated risk exposure more comprehensively.
C incorrect, the corporate account also carries credit risk, which may be interrelated with the personal account&#039;s risk.
D incorrect, ignoring the positions of any connected party creates gaps in risk monitoring.


### 温习册出处

客户信贷风险
客户信贷风险指期货经纪行因客户没有履行其在期货合约下的责任而蒙受损失的风险。期货经纪行应就每名客户制定审慎的风险限额（包括交易及持仓限额），
以纾减客户信贷风险。在适当情况下，该风险限额应从整体角度顾及关连客户组别（见下文附注）内所有客户的情况。
附注：某个“关连客户组别”涵盖个人客户（属配偶、主事人／受益人或代理关系）、法团客户（属同一公司集团的成员，或由一名个人单独或与其配偶共同控制（35%或以上的表决权））及任何藉担保安排而在财务上有关连的期货经纪行的客户（或财务上由并非该期货经纪行客户的第三方担保的任何客户组别）。
风险限额应考虑多项因素，例如：客户于某单一期货合约或相关期货合约的总持仓或净持仓、保证金总额或净额要求，以及对手方设定或任何适用法例或规例订明的任何持仓限额。纾减客户信贷风险的其中一个关键是向客户收取足够的抵押品。保证金应符合拟进行的期货合约交易所适用的保证金。除已获得高级管理层批准外，期货经纪行应制定并严格执行强制平仓的政策及程序。
附注 1：期货经纪行经考虑客户状况、产品、市况或其自身的情况后，可施加较其对手方所设定的金额为高的保证金规定。
附注 2：对手方包括任何执行代理人、结算代理人、执行或结算期货合约的交易所或结算所。
附注 3：上述讨论不适用于优惠保证金待遇。

## 第3题 (ID: 190792)

### 中文题目

一家期货公司开始处理涉及实物交收的铜期货合约。为管理相关风险，该公司应确保其相关人员具备哪方面的知识？
A. 仅需了解如何通过交易系统平仓
B. 应具备关于实物交收各方的角色、储存交付安排及相关行政规定的足够知识
C. 仅需熟悉铜的现货市场价格
D. 仅需了解期货合约的保证金计算方式


### English

A futures company begins handling copper futures contracts involving physical delivery. To manage the associated risks, what knowledge should the company ensure its relevant personnel possess?
A. Only need to know how to close positions through the trading system.
B. Should possess sufficient knowledge about the roles of parties involved in physical delivery,storage and delivery arrangements,and relevant administrative regulations.
C. Only need to be familiar with the spot market price of copper.
D. Only need to understand how futures contract margins are calculated.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，实物交收涉及复杂的物流和行政流程，仅了解平仓操作远远不够。
B正确，处理商品期货实物交收的期货经纪行，应具备足够的相关知识，包括参与各方的角色、有关储存及交付安排，以及相关行政规定。
C错误，了解现货价格是必要的，但不能替代对实物交收具体流程和规定的理解。
D错误，保证金计算是财务风险控制的一部分，但实物交收涉及操作和合规风险，需要专门的知识。


### Explanation

A incorrect, physical delivery involves complex logistical and administrative processes; knowing only how to close positions is far from sufficient.
B correct, a futures brokerage handling physical delivery of commodity futures should possess sufficient relevant knowledge, including the roles of the parties involved, storage and delivery arrangements, and relevant administrative regulations.
C incorrect, understanding spot prices is necessary but cannot substitute for knowledge of the specific processes and regulations of physical delivery.
D incorrect, margin calculation is part of financial risk control, but physical delivery involves operational and compliance risks that require specialized knowledge.


### 温习册出处

商品期货买卖风险限额
期货经纪行应备存一份其可交易的商品期货清单，该清单应已获得其高级管理层批准。
处理商品期货实物交收的期货经纪行应具备足够的相关知识，包括参与各方的角色、有关储存及交付安排，以及相关行政规定的知识。

## 第4题 (ID: 190546)

### 中文题目

市调机制和短暂停牌机制都是为了应对市场的剧烈波动。以下关于这两种机制适用时段的主要区别，哪一项是正确的？
A. 市调机制仅适用于开市前时段，而短暂停牌机制适用于持续交易时段。
B. 市调机制适用于所有交易时段，而短暂停牌机制仅适用于收市后交易时段。
C. 市调机制和短暂停牌机制都只适用于收市后交易时段。
D. 市调机制仅适用于持续交易时段，而短暂停牌机制适用于所有交易时段。


### English

Both the Market Volatility Control Mechanism and the Short-term Suspension Mechanism are designed to address sharp market fluctuations. Which of the following correctly describes a key difference in the trading sessions to which these two mechanisms primarily apply?
A. The Market Volatility Control Mechanism applies only to the Pre-opening Session,while the Short-term Suspension Mechanism applies to the Continuous Trading Session.
B. The Market Volatility Control Mechanism applies to all trading sessions,while the Short-term Suspension Mechanism applies only to the After-hours Trading Session.
C. Both the Market Volatility Control Mechanism and the Short-term Suspension Mechanism apply only to the After-hours Trading Session.
D. The Market Volatility Control Mechanism applies only to the Continuous Trading Session,while the Short-term Suspension Mechanism applies to all trading sessions.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误。市调机制适用于相关合约的正常交易时段（通常包括持续交易时段），并非仅适用于开市前时段。
B正确。根据规则描述，市调机制适用于“订明的合约”（未限定于特定时段，通常理解为主要交易时段）。而短暂停牌机制规则明确其适用于“在收市后交易时段”。
C错误。市调机制并非仅适用于收市后交易时段。
D错误。短暂停牌机制明确仅适用于收市后交易时段，而非所有时段。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The Market Volatility Control Mechanism applies to the normal trading sessions (typically including the Continuous Trading Session) for the relevant contracts, not only to the Pre-opening Session.
B is correct. Based on the rule description, the Market Volatility Control Mechanism applies to "prescribed contracts" (not limited to a specific session, commonly understood as the main trading sessions). The rules for the Short-term Suspension Mechanism explicitly state it applies "during the After-hours Trading Session".
C is incorrect. The Market Volatility Control Mechanism does not apply only to the After-hours Trading Session.
D is incorrect. The Short-term Suspension Mechanism explicitly applies only to the After-hours Trading Session, not all sessions.


### 温习册出处

期交所可订明一份受制于市场波动调节机制（“市调机制”）的合约清单。有关订明的合约，如该合约价格偏离市调机制参考价的市调机制百分比的价格，则会触发冷静期。可触发的冷静期次数上限以及冷静期的时间长短由期交所不时规定。冷静期完结后，该订明合约将恢复正常买卖。
期交所可订明一份受制于短暂停牌机制的合约清单。在收市后交易时段，如该合约价格偏离短暂停牌机制参考价的短暂停牌机制百分比的价格，则会触发短暂停牌。在短暂停牌时，相关合约所输入的买卖盘的余下未配对部分，将不作配对，及没有买卖盘可输入 HKATS 电子交易系统。

## 第5题 (ID: 190832)

### 中文题目

持牌公司与客户的协议载明了双方信息、服务费用及平仓条件，但缺少变更通知承诺和保证金逾期未付的监管报告说明。以下哪些内容缺失？
I. 双方全名及地址。
II. 资料变更通知承诺。
III. 服务内容及费用说明。
IV. 保证金逾期未付的监管报告说明。
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


### English

The agreement between a licensed firm and its client includes both parties&#039; information, service fees, and close-out conditions, but lacks the commitment to notify of changes and the explanation of regulatory reporting for late margin payment. Which items are missing?
I. Full names and addresses of both parties.
II. Commitment to notify each other of material changes.
III. Detailed description of services and fees.
IV. Explanation of regulatory reporting for failure to pay margin on time.
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I错误，因为协议已明确包含双方经核实的全名及地址，此项内容完整无缺。
II正确，因为协议中未包含双方在重要资料发生变更时需要互相通知的承诺条款，此项内容缺失。
III错误，因为协议已对服务内容及相关费用作出了详细说明，此项内容完整无缺。
IV正确，因为协议未包含当客户未能及时支付保证金时，持牌人需向相关监管机构进行报告的声明，此项针对衍生品交易的必备内容缺失。


### Explanation

I incorrect, because the agreement explicitly includes the verified full names and addresses of both parties. This item is complete and not missing.
II correct, because the agreement lacks a clause committing both parties to notify each other of material changes in information. This item is missing.
III incorrect, because the agreement provides a detailed description of the services and related fees. This item is complete and not missing.
IV correct, because the agreement omits the required statement that the licensed person must report to relevant regulators if the client fails to pay margin on time. This essential content for derivative transactions is missing.


### 温习册出处

客户协议的基本内容
客户协议应至少载有以下内容：
客户及持牌人或注册人的全名及地址（客户的全名及地址须经可靠证据核实）；
客户及持牌人或注册人作出的承诺，表示如有关资料出现重要变更时均会通知对方；

衍生产品方面（包括期货合约或期权）：
(i) 应客户的要求提供相关产品的规格及任何章程或其他要约文件的声明；及
(ii) 详细解释收取保证金的程序（包括在甚么情况下无须客户同意而将客户的持仓平仓，以及客户如未及时支付保证金而须向期交所及证监会作出报告）的声明；

## 第6题 (ID: 190786)

### 中文题目

某持牌法团在年度自查中发现，其某些内部监控措施未能达到《内部监控指引》中的实质标准。虽然这不直接违法，但最可能导致什么实质性后果？
A. 导致公司所有交易立即无效
B. 引发证监会对其负责人“适当人选”资格的重新审视
C. 自动触发对客户的民事赔偿责任
D. 被媒体公开曝光并导致股价下跌


### English

A licensed corporation discovered during its annual self-inspection that some of its internal control measures failed to meet the substantive standards outlined in the Internal Control Guidelines. While this is not directly illegal, what is the most likely substantial consequence?
A. Render all of the company&#039;s transactions immediately void.
B. Prompt the SFC to reconsider the "fit and proper" status of its responsible persons.
C. Automatically trigger civil liability to clients.
D. Lead to media exposure and a decline in share price.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，内部监控的不足并不直接影响已发生交易的法律效力。
B正确，未能遵守指引中的实质条文，可能对持牌人或其负责人员继续持牌或注册的“适当人选”资格造成负面影响，这是最主要的合规后果。
C错误，民事赔偿责任通常基于具体的违约或侵权行为，而非笼统的内部监控不足。
D错误，媒体曝光和市场反应是可能的间接后果，但并非最直接或必然的监管后果。


### Explanation

A incorrect, deficiencies in internal controls do not directly affect the legal validity of completed transactions.
B correct, failure to comply with substantive provisions of the guidelines may negatively impact the assessment of whether the licensee or its responsible persons remain "fit and proper" to continue being licensed or registered. This is the primary compliance consequence.
C incorrect, civil liability typically arises from specific breaches or torts, not general internal control deficiencies.
D incorrect, media exposure and market reaction are possible indirect consequences but are not the most direct or inevitable regulatory outcome.


### 温习册出处

适用范围及后果
《内部监控指引》与守则一样没有法律效力，未能遵守该指引不会因触犯法例
而受到检控。然而，与守则一样，未能遵守《内部监控指引》的实质条文，可能
会对持牌人或注册人继续持牌或注册的适当人选资格造成负面影响。
证监会表明会以务实的态度应用《内部监控指引》或其他相关指引，换言之，证
监会将顾及现实的情况及实际的处境，不会强硬地或以官僚方式应用《内部监
控指引》。这种做法通常适用于小型企业，在这种情况下，证监会预期小型企业
不会有复杂的职能区分制度，而监察及内部审计部门亦不会聘请大量员工。如
小型企业实施其他监控措施，亦能弥补在规模及雇员数量上的不足，符合有关
要求。

## 第7题 (ID: 190943)

### 中文题目

证监会某部门正在对一家持牌证券公司进行定期的“现场审查”，重点检查其销售过程中的客户适当性管理以及公司的资本充足水平。同时，该部门另一组同事正在审阅多家持牌法团提交的财务季度报表，进行“非现场监察”。根据证监会部门职能，这些工作最可能由哪个部门或科室负责？
I. 这些工作属于“中介机构部”的整体职责范围。
II. 进行现场审查和非现场监察，是“中介机构监察科”的具体职能。
III. 检查公司资本充足水平属于监察持牌法团财政稳健性的范畴。
IV. 检查客户适当性管理属于制订与发牌有关政策的范畴。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

A department of the SFC is conducting a regular “on-site inspection” of a licensed securities company, focusing on its client suitability management during the sales process and the company&#039;s capital adequacy levels. At the same time, another team from the same department is reviewing the quarterly financial reports submitted by several licensed corporations, conducting “off-site monitoring.” According to the SFC&#039;s departmental functions, which department or section is most likely responsible for this work?
I. This work falls within the overall scope of responsibilities of the “Intermediaries Division.”
II. Conducting on-site inspections and off-site monitoring is a specific function of the “Intermediaries Supervision Department.”
III. Checking the company&#039;s capital adequacy levels falls within the scope of monitoring the financial soundness of licensed corporations.
IV. Checking client suitability management falls within the scope of formulating policies related to licensing.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确，因为现场审查、非现场监察及财政状况监察均属于对持牌法团进行持续监管的内容，这是中介机构部的核心职责。

The firm has an aggregate limit for preferential clients and reported to the SFC three days after exceeding it. Which aspects are non-compliant?
I. Client meets eligibility for preferential treatment.
II. Limits fully consider the client&#039;s financial situation.
III. Complying with the aggregate quantitative limit.
IV. Reporting to the SFC within one business day after exceeding the limit.
A. I,II

### 温习册出处

证金待遇，即客户无须遵守执行或结算期货合约的交易所或结算所的规则所订
明的预缴抵押品规定。然而，如欲获提供优惠保证金待遇，客户必须符合上述规

守有关规定的情况下，优惠保证金待遇方会适用于该客户。对于有关客户，交易
限额应充分考虑客户的具体情况，包括其财务状况、清缴纪录、投资目标及风险
胃纳。
附注：提供优惠保证金待遇的期货经纪行亦须遵守适用于所有获得优惠待遇的
客户的公司整体量化限额。如超出该限额，必须于一个营业日内通知证监会。

## 第29题 (ID: 190959)

### 中文题目

李小姐在学习香港市场结构时注意到，股票期权和期货合约的期权都是期权类衍生工具，但在市场分类上常被区别对待。以下哪种说法最准确地反映了这种市场惯例的区别？
A. 两者都在同一交易所（联交所）买卖，因此被统一管理。
B. 股票期权在期交所（衍生工具市场）买卖，而期货期权在联交所（现货市场）买卖。
C. 股票期权在联交所（现货市场）买卖，而期货合约的期权在期交所（衍生工具市场）买卖。
D. 两者都被视为现货产品，因为都涉及基础资产的未来交割。


### English

While studying the structure of the Hong Kong market, Ms. Li noted that both stock options and options on futures contracts are option-type derivatives but are often treated differently in market classification. Which of the following statements most accurately reflects this distinction in market practice?
A. Both are traded on the same exchange (the Stock Exchange) and are therefore regulated uniformly.
B. Stock options are traded on the Futures Exchange (the derivatives market),while options on futures are traded on the Stock Exchange (the cash market).
C. Stock options are traded on the Stock Exchange (the cash market),while options on futures contracts are traded on the Futures Exchange (the derivatives market).
D. Both are considered cash products because they involve the future delivery of an underlying asset.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，因为两者并非在同一交易所买卖。股票期权在联交所交易，而期货合约的期权在期交所交易。
B错误，该描述与事实相反。实际情况是股票期权在联交所买卖，期货期权在期交所买卖。
C正确，这准确地反映了香港市场的实际情况和异常之处：股票期权及认股权证在联交所（常被称为“现货市场”）买卖，而期货合约的期权在期交所（常被称为“衍生工具市场”）买卖。
D错误，两者均非现货产品，而是衍生工具。此外，期权赋予的是权利而非交割义务，且该选项的描述与市场分类的实际情况不符。


### Explanation

A is incorrect because they are not traded on the same exchange. Stock options are traded on the Stock Exchange, while options on futures contracts are traded on the Futures Exchange.
B is incorrect because this description is the opposite of the facts. The reality is that stock options are traded on the Stock Exchange, and options on futures are traded on the Futures Exchange.
C is correct. This accurately reflects the actual situation and one of the anomalies of the Hong Kong market: stock options and warrants are traded on the Stock Exchange (often called the “cash market”), while options on futures contracts are traded on the Futures Exchange (often called the “derivatives market”).
D is incorrect. Neither are cash products; both are derivatives. Furthermore, options confer a right, not an obligation to deliver, and the description in this option does not match the reality of market classification.


### 温习册出处

衍生工具的释义
《证券及期货条例》附表 1 将“期货合约”定义为(a)根据期货市场的规则或惯例订立的合约，或该合约的期权；及(b)《证券及期货条例》第 392 条提述的公告订明为按照该公告的条款视为期货合约的权益、权利或财产。因此，对本章有重要相关性的产品为符合“期货合约”定义的产品，其中包括：
(a) 差价合约（符合期货合约或期货合约期权释义的合约）；及
(b) 期货合约及期权合约（指期货合约的期权，并在交易所买卖）。
务请注意，股票期权及认股权证一般均被视为衍生工具，因此将其与期货等其他衍生工具一起研究会更顺理成章。然而，股票期权及认股权证于香港联合交易所有限公司（“联交所”）进行买卖，而本温习手册则讨论于香港期货交易所有限公司（“期交所”）买卖的期货合约的期权。这情况与联交所常被称为“现货市场”而期交所常被称为“衍生工具市场”的情况有所不同，这点为香港市场的异常情况之一。

## 第30题 (ID: 190897)

### 中文题目

一家注册机构从事两类受规管活动。关于其“主管人员”的委任，以下哪项符合规定？
A. 它可以委任同一名主管人员同时监督两类活动，只要该人员有足够能力。
B. 它必须为每类受规管活动委任至少2名主管人员，因此总共至少需要4名主管人员。
C. 它必须就每一类受规管活动委任至少2名主管人员，但同一个人可以被委任为多类活动的主管人员，只要满足每类活动的监督要求。
D. 它只需要委任2名主管人员来监督所有受规管活动，无论活动类别数量。


### English

A registered institution engages in two types of regulated activities. Regarding the appointment of its "managers-in-charge," which of the following complies with the regulations?
A. It may appoint the same manager-in-charge to supervise both types of activities simultaneously,as long as that person has sufficient capability.
B. It must appoint at least two managers-in-charge for each type of regulated activity,therefore requiring a total of at least four managers-in-charge.
C. It must appoint at least two managers-in-charge for each type of regulated activity,but the same person can be appointed as a manager-in-charge for multiple types of activities,as long as the supervisory requirements for each activity are met.
D. It only needs to appoint two managers-in-charge to supervise all regulated activities,regardless of the number of activity types.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，要求是每类活动委任至少2名主管人员。仅委任1名主管人员，即使监督多类活动，也不满足“至少2名”的要求。
B错误，虽然每类活动需要至少2名主管人员，但同一个人可以被委任为多类活动的主管人员（即可以兼任）。因此，主管人员的总数可能少于活动类别数量乘以2。
C正确，注册机构须就每一类受规管活动委任至少2名主管人员。法律并未禁止同一个人担任多类活动的主管人员，只要该机构能够确保每类活动都有至少1名主管人员可时刻监督业务（并且总人数满足至少2名每类活动）。兼任是允许的，但需满足所有要求。
D错误，如果只有两类活动，委任2名主管人员可能无法满足“每类活动至少2名”的要求，除非这2人同时被委任为两类活动的主管人员，并且满足时刻监督的要求。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The requirement is to appoint at least two managers-in-charge for each type of activity. Appointing only one manager-in-charge, even if they supervise multiple activity types, does not satisfy the "at least two" requirement.
B is incorrect. Although each type of activity requires at least two managers-in-charge, the same person can be appointed as a manager-in-charge for multiple types of activities (i.e., they can hold concurrent appointments). Therefore, the total number of managers-in-charge may be less than the number of activity types multiplied by two.
C is correct. A registered institution must appoint at least two managers-in-charge for each type of regulated activity. The law does not prohibit the same person from acting as a manager-in-charge for multiple types of activities, provided the institution can ensure that for each activity type, at least one manager-in-charge is available to supervise the business at all times (and the total number satisfies the at least two per activity type requirement). Concurrent appointments are permissible, but all requirements must be met.
D is incorrect. If there are only two types of activities, appointing only two managers-in-charge may not satisfy the "at least two per activity type" requirement, unless these two individuals are concurrently appointed as managers-in-charge for both activity types and the requirement for at all times supervision is met.


### 温习册出处

有关人士及主管人员
就注册机构而言，等同于持牌代表及负责人员者分别是“有关人士”及“主管人员”。从事受规管活动的有关人士必须名列于金管局为有关人士备存的纪录册中。注册机构须就每一类受规管活动委任至少 2 名主管人员，且须有至少 1 名主管人员可时刻监督有关受规管活动的业务。
《证券及期货条例》附表 1 对不同实体的主管人员有不同的定义，分别指︰
(a) 持牌法团的负责人员；
(b) 注册机构的主管人员（见下文附注）；或
(c) 中介人的有联系实体中直接负责监管该有联系实体收取或持有该中介人的客户资产的董事。
附注︰注册机构须就每一类受规管活动委任至少 2 名主管人员，且须有至少 1名主管人员可时刻监督有关受规管活动的业务。

## 第32题 (ID: 190916)

### 中文题目

根据《财政资源规则》的要求，持牌法团在编制用于监管查核的财务资料时，必须确保其：
A. 反映交易的实质情况，而非仅仅是其法律形式。
B. 以交收日期为准，因为这是资金实际发生转移的时点。
C. 仅包含在资产负债表上明确列示的资产和负债。
D. 采用能最大化报告利润的会计政策。


### English

According to the requirements of the Financial Resources Rules, when a licensed corporation prepares financial information for regulatory inspection, it must ensure that:
A. It reflects the substance of transactions,rather than merely their legal form.
B. It is based on the settlement date,as this is the point at which funds are actually transferred.
C. It only includes assets and liabilities explicitly presented on the balance sheet.
D. It adopts accounting policies that maximize reported profits.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A正确，监管机构要求采用的基准必须能反映交易的实质情况，这要求超越单纯的法律形式，关注其经济实质。
B错误，规则要求个别项目须于交易日期（而非交收日期）计算。
C错误，未于资产负债表中呈列的负债一般也须计算在内，除非获得证监会豁除。
D错误，采用的会计原则必须是被普遍接纳的，目的是真实公允地反映状况，而非最大化利润。


### Explanation

A is correct. The regulatory authority requires that the basis adopted must be able to reflect the substance of transactions, which demands looking beyond mere legal form to focus on economic substance.
B is incorrect. The rules require that individual items must be calculated on the trade date (rather than the settlement date).
C is incorrect. Liabilities not presented in the balance sheet generally must also be taken into account, unless excluded by the SFC.
D is incorrect. The accounting principles adopted must be generally accepted, with the aim of presenting a true and fair view of the position, not maximizing profits.


### 温习册出处

简单而言，持牌法团必须拥有充足的速动资产用作偿还债务（除股份及债务证券或其他长期贷款外，多属短期项目）。
资产负债表的资料可用于查核持牌法团的财政健全状况。就《财政资源规则》而言，监管机构要求根据下列特定基准编制或调整该等资料：
(a) 须按照普遍接纳的会计原则编制；
(b) 采用的基准必须能反映交易的实质情况；
(c) 个别项目必须于交易日期（而非交收日期）计算；及
(d) 未于资产负债表中呈列的负债一般须计算在内，除非证监会同意可豁除有关负债。

## 第33题 (ID: 190864)

### 中文题目

“南方证券”持有客户张先生的款项于指定的客户独立账户中。南方证券在何种情况下，可以将该款项从独立账户中转出？
I. 张先生亲临柜台，要求提取现金。
II. 张先生通过其有效常设授权书授权的投资经理，发出指令将款项转入某个特定投资产品。
III. 南方证券因自身运营资金紧张，临时借用该笔款项，并承诺在一周内归还。
IV. 用于偿还张先生因融资交易而对南方证券产生的贷款利息欠款。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

"Southern Securities" holds client Mr. Zhang&#039;s money in a designated separate client account. Under which of the following circumstances may Southern Securities transfer this money out of the separate account?
I. Mr. Zhang visits the counter in person and requests cash withdrawal.
II. Mr. Zhang&#039;s investment manager, authorized under his valid standing authorization, issues an instruction to transfer the money to a specific investment product.
III. Southern Securities, due to its own tight operational funds, temporarily borrows this sum of money and promises to repay it within one week.
IV. To repay the loan interest debt that Mr. Zhang owes to Southern Securities due to margin trading.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确，客户亲自要求提取其名下资金，是所有权人行使财产权利的最直接方式，中介人予以执行是履行其保管和支付义务。II正确，常设授权书是客户预先设定的、持续有效的指令。经其授权的投资经理根据授权条款发出操作指令，应被视为客户意志的延伸，中介人据此执行符合预先约定的代理权限。III错误，中介人将客户资金用于自身运营周转，无论是否承诺归还，都实质上是将客户资产用于中介人自身目的。这违反了客户资产必须与中介人自有资产严格分离并仅为客户利益而持有的基本原则。IV正确，贷款利息是客户对中介人负有的明确金钱债务。使用客户账户内的资金偿还该债务，是了结双方之间该笔特定债权债务关系的直接方式。


### Explanation

I is correct. A client personally requesting withdrawal of funds in their name is the most direct way for the owner to exercise property rights. The intermediary executing this request fulfills its custodial and payment duties. II is correct. A standing authorization is a pre-set, continuously effective instruction from the client. Operational instructions issued by an investment manager authorized under its terms should be regarded as an extension of the client&#039;s will. The intermediary acting accordingly complies with the pre-agreed scope of agency authority. III is incorrect. An intermediary using client funds for its own operational needs, regardless of any repayment promise, essentially uses client assets for the intermediary&#039;s own purposes. This violates the fundamental principle that client assets must be strictly segregated from the intermediary&#039;s own assets and held solely for the client&#039;s benefit. IV is correct. Loan interest is a clear monetary debt the client owes to the intermediary. Using funds from the client&#039;s account to repay this debt is a direct method to settle that specific creditor-debtor relationship between the two parties.


### 温习册出处

从独立帐户提取款项
持牌法团或有联系实体如持有客户款项于独立帐户内，须将该客户款项一直保留在该帐户内，直至以下情况出现为止：
(a) 向有关客户支付该款项；
(b) 用于履行该客户就该持牌法团代其支付的关于交收或保证金规定的义务的款项；
(c) 用以支付该客户对持牌法团或有联系实体的欠款；
(d) 按照该客户书面指示支付该款项；或
(e) 按照该客户常设授权支付该款项。

### 中文题目

在判断一项服务是否构成需要持牌的“自动化交易服务”时，参与者的目的是一项重要考量。以下哪些关于该目的的陈述是准确的？
