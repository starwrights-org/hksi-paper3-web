## 第1题 (ID: 190831)

### 中文题目

一位投资者在一家证券公司（非期交所参与者）开户进行股票交易。该公司提供的客户协议中，包含了一项关于“赔偿基金承担有限责任”的条款。投资者质疑为何会有此条款，因为该公司并不交易期货。对此，应如何解释？
A. 此条款适用于所有持牌人，必须包含
B. 此条款通常只适用于作为期交所参与者的持牌人，出现在非期货公司的协议中可能不合适
C. 此条款是标准模板的一部分，无需深究
D. 此条款是为了保护公司利益，应予以保留


### English

An investor opens an account for stock trading with a securities firm (which is not an HKFE Exchange Participant). The client agreement provided by the firm includes a clause about “the Compensation Fund assuming limited liability.” The investor questions why this clause is present since the firm does not trade futures. How should this be explained?
A. This clause applies to all licensees and must be included.
B. This clause is typically required only for licensees who are HKFE Exchange Participants; its presence in a non-futures firm&#039;s agreement may be inappropriate.
C. This clause is part of a standard template and requires no further scrutiny.
D. This clause is for the protection of the firm&#039;s interests and should be retained.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，“赔偿基金承担有限责任”的声明是期交所参与者的客户协议必须包含的额外条文，并非适用于所有持牌人。
B正确，解释赔偿基金有限责任的条款，是专门针对作为期交所参与者的持牌人的要求。对于不从事期货交易（非期交所参与者）的证券公司，其客户协议原则上无需包含此特定条款。
C错误，客户协议应根据持牌人具体业务类型来拟定条款，不应盲目套用模板。
D错误，条款的合理性基于其是否符合相关规定，而非单方面保护某一方。


### Explanation

A incorrect, the statement about the Compensation Fund&#039;s limited liability is an additional clause specifically required in client agreements for HKFE Exchange Participants, not for all licensees.
B correct, the clause explaining the limited liability of the Compensation Fund is a specific requirement for licensees who are HKFE Exchange Participants. For a securities firm that does not engage in futures trading (not an Exchange Participant), this specific clause is generally not required in its client agreement.
C incorrect, client agreements should be drafted according to the specific business type of the licensee, not by blindly applying templates.
D incorrect, the appropriateness of a clause depends on its compliance with relevant rules, not solely on protecting one party&#039;s interests.

## 第3题 (ID: 190862)

### 中文题目

“南方证券”根据与客户李女士的书面协议，有权保留其独立账户中资金所生利息的20%。该账户于6月10日产生了一笔利息，但南方证券的系统直到6月12日才生成报告显示该笔利息已计入账户贷方。南方证券负责人员于6月12日上午审阅了该报告。南方证券应最迟于何时将其保留的20%利息从李女士的独立账户中转出？
A. 6月10日，即利息产生日。
B. 6月12日，即系统报告生成且负责人员察觉之日。
C. 6月13日结束前。
D. 6月11日，即利息产生后的下一个营业日。


### English

Southern Securities, based on a written agreement with client Ms. Li, is entitled to retain 20% of the interest generated from funds in her separate account. The account generated interest on June 10, but Southern Securities&#039; system did not generate a report showing the interest had been credited to the account until June 12. The responsible officer at Southern Securities reviewed the report on the morning of June 12. By when must Southern Securities transfer out the 20% interest it retains from Ms. Li&#039;s separate account at the latest?
A. June 10,the day the interest was generated.
B. June 12,the day the system report was generated and the responsible officer became aware.
C. By the end of June 13.
D. June 11,the next business day after the interest was generated.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，利息“产生日”与“记入账户贷方”或“被察觉”的时间点可能不同。时限计算的触发点是后者。B错误，6月12日是“察觉”日，但规定的发放时限是“察觉后一个营业日内”，这意味着最晚可以在下一个营业日（6月13日）结束前完成，而非必须在察觉当日。C正确，时限从“利息记入该帐户的贷方”或“持牌法团察觉该利息已记入该帐户的贷方”两者中以较迟者为准开始计算，其后一个营业日内必须发放。本例中，利息于10日记入贷方，但12日才被察觉，较迟者是12日（察觉日）。因此，发放须在13日（察觉后的一个营业日）结束前完成。D错误，6月11日是利息产生后（假设10日为产生日）的下一个营业日，但此时中介人可能尚未察觉利息已入账。时限的起算依赖于“记入贷方”或“察觉”的确认，而非简单的产生日期。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The date the interest is "generated" may differ from the time it is "credited to the account" or "becomes aware." The trigger for timeline calculation is the latter. B is incorrect. June 12 is the "awareness" date, but the stipulated disbursement timeline is "within one business day after becoming aware," meaning it can be completed by the end of the next business day (June 13) at the latest, not necessarily on the awareness day itself. C is correct. The timeline is calculated from the later of two events: "the interest is credited to the account" or "the licensed corporation becomes aware the interest has been credited," and disbursement must occur within one business day thereafter. In this example, the interest was credited on the 10th but only became aware on the 12th; the later date is the 12th (awareness). Therefore, disbursement must be completed by the end of the 13th (one business day after awareness). D is incorrect. June 11 is the next business day after the interest was generated (assuming the 10th was the generation date), but the intermediary may not have been aware the interest was credited by then. The start of the timeline depends on confirmation of "crediting" or "awareness," not simply the generation date.


### 温习册出处

利息
除非另有书面协定，否则因持有客户款项于独立帐户而收取的利息应拨归客户。如持牌法团或其有联系实体根据与客户订立的书面协议而有权保留任何部分的该利息款额，则该持牌法团或其有联系实体必须在：
(a) 该利息记入该帐户的贷方；或
(b) 该持牌法团或其有联系实体察觉该利息已记入该帐户的贷方，（两者以较迟者为准）后一个营业日内，从该帐户发放该利息款额。

I. 消除彼此之间的分歧并改善监管效率。

A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

The Memorandum of Understanding signed between the SFC and HKEX aims to achieve which of the following objectives?
I. To eliminate differences between them and improve regulatory efficiency.
II. To enable both parties to better monitor and address any cross-market risks.
III. To take appropriate actions in the public interest or for maintaining the fair and orderly operation of the market.
IV. To formally incorporate HKEX into the sequence of government regulatory agencies, granting it statutory enforcement powers.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确，因为谅解备忘录可以消除彼此之间的分歧并改善监管效率。
II正确，因为谅解备忘录有助于双方更妥善地监察及应付任何跨市场风险。

I is correct because an MOU can eliminate differences between the parties and improve regulatory efficiency.

## 第5题 (ID: 190834)

### 中文题目

一对夫妇开设了一个投资联名账户，其中丈夫表示对期货交易很了解，但妻子从未接触过。当他们通过该账户首次申请交易股指期货时，持牌机构应如何评估其衍生工具知识水平？
A. 只需评估丈夫的知识水平，因为他是主要联系人
B. 应评估实际操作或发出交易指示的人士（例如丈夫）是否具备衍生工具知识
C. 需要夫妇双方都通过衍生工具知识测试
D. 无需评估，因为联名账户视作一个实体


### English

A couple opens a joint investment account. The husband states he is knowledgeable about futures trading, but the wife has no exposure to it. When they apply to trade stock index futures for the first time through this account, how should the licensed firm assess their level of derivative knowledge?
A. Only assess the husband&#039;s knowledge level,as he is the primary contact.
B. Should assess whether the person who actually operates or gives trading instructions (e.g.,the husband) possesses knowledge of derivatives.
C. Both spouses need to pass a derivative knowledge test.
D. No assessment needed,as a joint account is treated as a single entity.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，评估应针对实际决策或操作者。
B正确，对于联名账户，应评估发出买卖盘指示或操作该账户的人士是否具备衍生工具知识。
C错误，规定并未要求必须通过形式化的“测试”，而是进行适当评估。
D错误，联名账户仍需遵守投资者分类规定。


### Explanation

A incorrect, the assessment should target the actual decision-maker or operator.
B correct, for a joint account, the assessment should focus on whether the person placing orders or operating the account possesses knowledge of derivatives.
C incorrect, the rules do not require passing a formalized “test” but rather conducting an appropriate assessment.
D incorrect, joint accounts are still subject to investor classification rules.

## 第6题 (ID: 190631)

### 中文题目

某虚拟资产平台为来自反洗钱制度薄弱国家的非居民客户提供匿名交易服务，并通过网络渠道建立业务关系。该平台将此类客户统一归类为低风险。根据《打击洗钱指引》附录A中的风险示例，该平台的风险分类可能存在哪些问题？
I. 对国家风险因素评估不当。
II. 对客户风险因素评估不当。
III. 对产品/服务风险因素评估不当。
IV. 对交付渠道风险因素评估不当。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

A virtual asset platform provides anonymous trading services to non-resident clients from countries with weak anti-money laundering systems and establishes business relationships through online channels. The platform uniformly categorizes such clients as low-risk. According to the risk examples in Appendix A of the Anti-Money Laundering Guidelines, what issues might exist in the platform&#039;s risk classification?
I. Improper assessment of country risk factors.
II. Improper assessment of client risk factors.
III. Improper assessment of product/service risk factors.
IV. Improper assessment of delivery channel risk factors.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确。客户来自反洗钱制度薄弱的国家，属于较高国家风险，归类为低风险不当。
II正确。无明确原因在香港开户的非居民客户属于较高客户风险，归类为低风险不当。
III正确。匿名交易服务属于较高产品/服务风险，归类为低风险不当。
IV错误。通过非面对面方式（网络渠道）建立业务关系属于较高交付渠道风险，但题目中未说明该平台是否因此调整了风险评级，且其风险分类是基于前三项因素，故无法直接判断此项评估是否不当。


### Explanation

I correct. Clients from countries with weak anti-money laundering systems represent higher country risk; classifying them as low-risk is improper.
II correct. Non-resident clients opening accounts in Hong Kong without clear reasons represent higher client risk; classifying them as low-risk is improper.
III correct. Anonymous trading services represent higher product/service risk; classifying them as low-risk is improper.
IV incorrect. Establishing business relationships through non-face-to-face means (online channels) represents higher delivery channel risk. However, the question does not specify whether the platform adjusted its risk rating accordingly, and its classification appears based on the first three factors, so this cannot be directly judged as improper assessment.
