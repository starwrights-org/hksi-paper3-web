## 第1题 (ID: 190877)

### 中文题目

金管局在对“宏达金融”进行现场检查时，发现该公司作为中介人，在与第三方签订的协议中，授予对方在特定条件下“处置客户证券抵押品的广泛权利”。检查员指出这可能存在问题，主要因为《客户证券规则》对以下哪方面有明确限制？
A. 中介人可向客户收取的托管费费率上限。
B. 处置有关证券及证券抵押品的权利范围。
C. 证券交易系统中订单执行的最优价格偏离幅度。
D. 客户证券账户开立所需的最短文件审核时间。


### English

During an on-site inspection of "Grand Finance," the HKMA found that the company, as an intermediary, had granted a third party "broad rights to dispose of client securities collateral" under certain conditions in an agreement. The inspector pointed out this could be problematic, primarily because the Client Securities Rules impose clear limitations on which of the following aspects?
A. The maximum fee rate an intermediary can charge clients for custodial services.
B. The scope of rights regarding the disposal of such securities and securities collateral.
C. The permissible deviation from the best execution price for orders in the securities trading system.
D. The minimum document review time required for opening a client securities account.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，托管费率属于商业收费安排，并非《客户证券规则》规范的核心。B正确，因为《客户证券规则》明确包括了“处置有关证券及证券抵押品的权利范围”。规则旨在限定和明确处置权利的条件和边界，防止权利的滥用，从而保障客户资产安全。“授予广泛权利”的表述可能违反了规则对权利范围必须清晰、有限的规定。C错误，订单执行价格属于交易最佳执行范畴，与资产处置权利无关。D错误，账户开立审核时间属于反洗钱或操作效率问题，与资产处置权利的界定无关。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. Custodial fee rates are a commercial charging arrangement and are not the core regulated by the Client Securities Rules. B is correct because the Client Securities Rules explicitly include “the scope of rights regarding the disposal of such securities and securities collateral.” The rules aim to define and limit the conditions and boundaries of disposal rights to prevent their abuse and thereby protect client assets. The phrase "granting broad rights" likely contravenes the rule&#039;s requirement that the scope of rights must be clear and limited. C is incorrect. Order execution prices pertain to best execution and are unrelated to asset disposal rights. D is incorrect. Account opening review times relate to anti-money laundering or operational efficiency issues, not the definition of asset disposal rights.


### 温习册出处

客户证券
导言
《客户证券规则》载有规定中介人或其有联系实体处理在香港收取或持有的客户证券或证券抵押品的方式。
《客户证券规则》包括：
(a) 存放或登记客户证券及证券抵押品的规定；
(b) 获准提取证券的各种情况；及
(c) 处置有关证券及证券抵押品的权利范围。

## 第2题 (ID: 190808)

### 中文题目

期交所参与者将客户期交所/非期交所款项分账户管理，证券抵押品账户名称合规。但有时证券存入迟于一个营业日，且曾将自有现金临时存入客户账户。以下哪些方面不合规？
I. 客户款项分账户管理。
II. 证券抵押品账户名称。
III. 证券存入独立账户的时效。
IV. 独立账户的专用性。
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


### English

An HKFE participant separates client HKFE/non-HKFE funds into accounts and uses a compliant name for the securities collateral account. However, securities are sometimes deposited later than one business day, and firm cash was once temporarily deposited into a client account. Which aspects are non-compliant?
I. Segregated management of client funds.
II. Name of the securities collateral account.
III. Timeliness of depositing securities into the segregated account.
IV. Exclusivity of the segregated account.
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I错误，客户款项分账户管理符合要求。
II错误，证券抵押品账户名称符合要求。
III正确，证券未在收取后一个营业日内存入，不合规。
IV正确，将自有现金存入客户账户，违反账户专用性，不合规。


### Explanation

I incorrect, segregated management of client funds meets requirements.
II incorrect, the name of the securities collateral account meets requirements.
III correct, depositing securities later than one business day after receipt is non-compliant.
IV correct, depositing firm cash into a client account violates exclusivity and is non-compliant.


### 温习册出处

《操守准则》亦载有规管期交所交易所参与者有关客户资产的持牌人或注册人的特别规定。根据《操守准则》，他们：
(a) 应透过至少两个独立银行帐户妥善保管各客户的款项，以分开期交所交易及非期交所交易的客户款项；
(b) 应透过开立银行或证券帐户，妥善保管各客户作为保证金规定抵押品而存放的证券，而有关帐户的名称应包含“客户”、“独立”、“非公司”或其他类似用语，将期交所交易及非期交所交易的客户证券抵押品互相分开；
(c) 应在独立帐户内持有来自客户（作为受托人）的所有款项及证券，将有关款项及证券与持牌人或注册人的资产分开；
(d) 应在切实可行的时间内及在任何情况下，在收取客户款项或证券后一个营业日内，尽快将客户的款项或证券存放入独立帐户内；
(e) 应确保客户的现金或证券存入独立帐户，而不属于客户的现金或证券则不会存入独立帐户（即独立帐户应仅用作保留客户的资产）；
(f) 可就以下目的提取客户的资产：
(i) 持牌人或注册人因应客户指示进行期货或期权业务而须履行结算所的责任；
(ii) 转拨予另一个独立银行帐户；及
(iii) 向客户付款或根据其指示须付予一个独立银行帐户，即使已向另一持牌人或注册人缴付资产。

## 第3题 (ID: 190761)

### 中文题目

银行职员李小姐在处理一位客户的大额现金存款时，注意到该客户资金来源不明且行为可疑。她想起该客户曾有被捕记录。根据《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》，李小姐此时最应立即采取的正确行动是什么？
A. 立即拒绝处理该笔存款，并要求客户离开。
B. 在合理范围内尽快，向她所属银行的指定洗钱报告官或联合财富情报组进行举报。
C. 为避免承担风险，先完成存款操作，但记录下客户信息以备后查。
D. 向关系较好的同事私下透露该客户的疑点，提醒他们注意。


### English

Bank employee Ms. Li is processing a large cash deposit from a customer. She notices the source of funds is unclear and the customer&#039;s behavior is suspicious, recalling the customer has a past arrest record. According to the Drug Trafficking (Recovery of Proceeds) Ordinance, what is the most immediate and correct action Ms. Li should take?
A. Immediately refuse to process the deposit and ask the customer to leave.
B. As soon as reasonably practicable,make a report to her bank&#039;s designated Money Laundering Reporting Officer or the Joint Financial Intelligence Unit.
C. To avoid risk,complete the deposit transaction first but record the customer&#039;s information for future reference.
D. Privately discuss her suspicions about the customer with close colleagues to alert them.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，单方面拒绝服务可能引发纠纷，且并非处理此类嫌疑的标准程序。
B正确，当怀疑资金与犯罪活动相关时，应在合理时间内向内部指定人员或特定执法单位报告，这是法定的责任。
C错误，处理可疑资金后再记录，违反了立即行动的原则，并可能使自身和机构面临法律风险。
D错误，私下传播此类信息可能构成“泄密”，干扰调查并可能违反保密规定。


### Explanation

A incorrect. Unilaterally refusing service may cause disputes and is not the standard procedure for handling such suspicions.
B correct. When suspecting funds are related to criminal activity, reporting to an internal designated person or a specific law enforcement unit within a reasonable time frame is a legal responsibility.
C incorrect. Processing suspicious funds before recording contradicts the principle of immediate action and may expose oneself and the institution to legal risks.
D incorrect. Privately disseminating such information may constitute "tipping off," interfering with investigations and potentially violating confidentiality rules.


### 温习册出处

《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》
《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》规定，任何人如处理其已知或相信为贩毒得益的财产，即属犯罪。“处理”一词包括：
(a) 收受或取得该财产；
(b) 隐藏或掩饰该财产；
(c) 处置或转换该财产；
(d) 把该财产运入香港或调离香港；及／或
(e) 以该财产借款或作保证。
《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》规定，任何人士如知悉或怀疑任何财产与贩毒有关，
须在合理范围内尽快向获授权人员举报。获授权人员包括：警务人员、海关人员
或由香港警方与香港海关成立并运作的联合财富情报组。此外，亦可向其雇主
就此目的而委派的人士作出举报。如未能披露即属犯法，惟可以有意披露，但有
合理原因不作披露为抗辩理由。
附注：任何向有关机构作出披露的人士均不被视为违反任何合约或专业责任，
亦无须就作出披露所引致的损失或损害承担责任。
任何人士如向他人披露已根据上文规定作出披露乃属犯罪。
《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》规定，任何人士如被控贩毒，其财产可被发出限制令
及押记令。接获该等命令的持牌法团、注册机构及持牌虚拟资产服务提供者，可
能须按指令交付文件或资料。如未能交付，即属犯罪。

## 第4题 (ID: 190775)

### 中文题目

公司开普通账户时合规，披露充分。开委托账户时订立协议、提供结单、划分决策与交易，但无独立员工定期检讨账户表现。以下哪些方面不足？
I. 遵守认识你的客户原则。
II. 向客户充分披露费用和风险。
III. 订立载明投资目的和策略的委托账户协议。
IV. 指定独立员工定期检讨委托账户表现。
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


### English

The firm complies with KYC for regular accounts and provides full disclosure. For discretionary accounts, it has agreements, provides statements, separates decision-making from trading, but lacks independent staff to regularly review account performance. Which aspects are deficient?
I. Adhering to know-your-client principles.
II. Fully disclosing fees and risks to clients.
III. Entering into discretionary account agreements specifying investment objectives and strategies.
IV. Designating independent staff to regularly review discretionary account performance.
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I错误，因为公司在开立普通账户时已严格遵守认识你的客户原则，并记录了所有相关资料，这表明其在此项基本要求上不存在不足。
II错误，因为公司已向客户充分披露了费用、风险及相关权利等信息，满足了信息透明度的要求，因此此方面不存在不足。
III正确，因为公司已为委托账户订立了明确载列投资目的、策略及酌情权条款的协议，这符合委托账户管理的基本要求，因此此方面不存在不足。
IV正确，因为公司未指定独立于该账户的员工定期检讨委托账户的表现。定期且独立的检讨是监督委托账户操作、评估其是否符合既定策略和客户目标的关键监控措施，此项缺失构成不足。


### Explanation

I incorrect, because the firm strictly adheres to KYC principles and records all relevant information when opening regular accounts, indicating no deficiency in this basic requirement.
II incorrect, because the firm fully discloses fees, risks, and related rights to clients, meeting the requirement for transparency, so there is no deficiency in this aspect.
III correct, because the firm has entered into discretionary account agreements that clearly specify investment objectives, strategies, and discretionary terms, meeting the basic requirement for managing such accounts, so there is no deficiency here.
IV correct, because the firm does not designate staff independent of the accounts to regularly review the performance of discretionary accounts. Regular and independent review is a key control measure to monitor operations and assess compliance with strategies and client objectives. This omission constitutes a deficiency.

《内部监控指引》的附录载有适用于运作监控与风险管理范畴的建议监控措施及程序。

开立及处理客户帐户
以下是获普遍采用的建议内部监控措施及程序，但非强制规定。证监会认为，各
中介人应建立于各情况下均属合理的措施。中介人在推行内部监控制度时，应
审慎考虑其本身公司的业务特质及个别需要。
开立帐户的程序应包括：
(a) 严格遵守“认识你的客户”原则；
(b) 记录所有相关的客户资料（确定实益拥有人的身份，以及客户的财务状况、投资经验和目的等）；
(c) 确保客户已具备充分的资料，例如有关中介人、中介人的服务、风险披露声明、收费性质及范围、可能征收的罚款及其他费用的资料；及
(d) 向客户提供有关其权利的资料，例如客户在投资者赔偿基金安排下所得到的保障等。

(a) 订立委托帐户协议，有关协议须载列客户的投资目的和策略，以及行使酌情权时须依据的有关条款；
(b) 由独立于有关帐户的指定员工定期检讨帐户的表现；
(c) 就帐户结余及交易详情，定期向客户提供结单和及时的特别报告；及
(d) 清楚划分投资决策程序及交易程序。

## 第5题 (ID: 190924)

### 中文题目

关于通过传媒（如电视台、报纸）发表金融市场意见的人士通常获得监管豁免的原因，以下哪些是合理的解释？
I. 这类人士的收入通常与他们所发表的具体意见或建议没有直接的经济联系。
II. 他们提供的建议是针对特定客户群体的个性化投资方案。
III. 他们的意见被视为向不特定的广大公众传播信息，而非提供个人化的投资顾问服务。
IV. 传媒机构在金融信息传播和教育公众方面扮演着重要角色。
A. I,II,III
B. I,III,IV
C. I,II,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

"Regarding the reasons why persons expressing opinions on financial markets through the media (such as television stations, newspapers) are generally granted regulatory exemptions, which of the following are reasonable explanations?
I. The income of such persons is generally not directly economically linked to the specific opinions or advice they give.
II. The advice they provide is a personalized investment plan targeted at a specific client group.
III. Their opinions are regarded as disseminating information to the unspecified general public, rather than providing personalized investment advisory services.
IV. Media organizations play an important role in the dissemination of financial information and public education."
A. I,II,III
B. I,III,IV
C. I,II,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I 正确，这类人士的收入与其所作的意见或建议通常没有直接联系，因此不被视为因提供具体建议而收取直接报酬。
II 错误，他们的建议是向普罗大众而非对特定实体或个人作出的，因此不属于针对性的投资建议。
III 正确，他们的意见是向普罗大众作出的，具有普遍性。
IV 正确，传媒作为资讯传播者，被认为对金融业有积极贡献。


### Explanation

I is correct. The income of such persons is generally not directly linked to the opinions or advice they give, so it is not considered direct remuneration for providing specific advice.
II is incorrect. Their advice is given to the general public, not to specific entities or individuals, and therefore does not constitute targeted investment advice.
III is correct. Their opinions are directed at the general public and are of a general nature.
IV is correct. The media, as disseminators of information, are considered to make a positive contribution to the financial industry.


### 温习册出处

对透过传媒提供意见的有关人士作出豁免符合大多数国际司法权区的做法。一般而言，该等人士的收入与所作的任何意见或建议并没有直接联系，因此，可以视作他们并没有收取直接的回报。况且，他们的建议是向普罗大众而非对特定实体作出的。传媒一般被视为资讯的传播者，对金融业贡献良多。
第 5 类受规管活动的牌照或注册规定适用于从事就期货合约提供意见业务的法团。有关规定适用于代表持牌法团履行受规管职能的所有员工，惟会计师、文员或出纳员除外。为认可财务机构履行就期货合约提供意见之受规管职能的人士须名列于金管局备存的纪录册上，并显示为就该类受规管活动受聘于该认可财务机构的人士。
因此，提供意见及接受交易指示（并获准执行客户指示）的任何持牌法团（或注册机构）的员工须分别就第 5 类及第 2 类受规管活动获发牌（或名列于金管局的纪录册上），除非有关员工完全因附带于第 2 类受规管活动而提供意见或分析，则另作别论。倘持牌法团（或注册机构）将就期货合约提供意见作为独立业务，则须取得第 5 类受规管活动的牌照或注册。只就期货合约提供意见而不接受交易指示的持牌法团代表（或注册机构）亦须取得第 5 类牌照（或名列于金管局的纪录册上）。

## 第6题 (ID: 190846)

### 中文题目

某持牌资产管理公司（从事第9类受规管活动）与一位高净值客户发生投资损失纠纷。客户要求通过调解中心解决，该公司是否必须参与调解？
A. 是，因为所有持牌人均受调解计划约束
B. 否，因为高净值客户争议不适用调解计划
C. 是，因为该公司并非从事第10类受规管活动的商号
D. 否，因为调解仅适用于个人客户


### English

A licensed asset management firm (engaged in Type 9 regulated activity) had an investment loss dispute with a high-net-worth client. The client requested resolution through the FDRC. Is the firm required to participate in mediation?
A. Yes,because all licensees are bound by the FDRS.
B. No,because disputes involving high-net-worth clients are not covered by the FDRS.
C. Yes,because the firm is not a Type 10 regulated activity firm.
D. No,because mediation only applies to individual clients.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，并非所有持牌人，根据《证券及期货条例》进行第10类受规管活动的商号除外。
B错误，调解计划的适用性基于机构类型和争议性质，并非单纯依据客户是否为高净值人士。
C正确，从事第9类受规管活动的持牌人（第10类除外）应全面遵从调解计划，必须参与。
D错误，调解计划的适用对象包括符合条件的个人及部分小企业客户，并非仅限个人客户。


### Explanation

A incorrect, not all licensees; firms engaged in Type 10 regulated activity under the SFO are exempt.
B incorrect, the applicability of the FDRS depends on the type of firm and the nature of the dispute, not solely on whether the client is a high-net-worth individual.
C correct, a licensee engaged in Type 9 regulated activity (except Type 10) should fully comply with the FDRS and must participate.
D incorrect, the FDRS covers eligible individual and some small business clients, not only individuals.


### 温习册出处

调解计划之下的责任

### 中文题目

B. 应该合并计算，因为Alpha公司进行整体风险监控，构成了对持仓的控制。
C. 不需要合并计算，只要Alpha公司不发出日常交易指示，就不构成对两家子公司具体持仓的控制。
D. 只需要在两家公司的持仓分别超过须申报水平后才考虑合并。


### English

Financial holding company Alpha has two subsidiaries: securities firm Beta and asset management company Gamma. Beta trades stock options for its proprietary account, while Gamma trades the same type of stock options for the funds it manages. Alpha monitors the risks of both subsidiaries on an overall basis but does not intervene in their daily trading decisions. According to the relevant rules, how should the options positions of Beta and Gamma be treated when calculating position limits?
A. Must be aggregated because they are affiliated enterprises under the same holding company.
B. Should be aggregated because Alpha&#039;s overall risk monitoring constitutes control over the positions.
C. Do not need to be aggregated,as long as Alpha does not issue daily trading instructions,it does not constitute control over the specific positions of the two subsidiaries.
D. Only need to consider aggregation after the positions of each company individually exceed the reporting level.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误。仅有关联企业关系本身，不足以触发合并计算的要求。关键在于是否存在规则定义的“控制”。
B错误。整体风险监控属于集团层面的风险管理职能，不等同于能独立于持仓拥有人（子公司）行使具体的买卖期权合约的酌情权。这不符合“控制”的定义。
C正确。规则明确，仅因企业架构（如母子公司关系）而获得控制权，但若无发出日常指示，则不视为控制相关合约持仓。因此，两家子公司的持仓应分别计算。
D错误。合并计算的适用性是基于对持仓的“持有或控制”关系，而非持仓规模是否超过某个水平。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. A mere affiliated enterprise relationship alone is insufficient to trigger the requirement for aggregation. The key lies in whether there is "control" as defined by the rules.
B is incorrect. Overall risk monitoring is a function of group-level risk management and does not equate to exercising independent discretion over specific buy/sell decisions for option contracts on behalf of the position holder (the subsidiaries). This does not meet the definition of "control."
C is correct. The rules explicitly state that control acquired solely by virtue of a corporate structure (such as a parent-subsidiary relationship) does not constitute control over the relevant contract positions, provided that no daily trading instructions are given. Therefore, the positions of the two subsidiaries should be calculated separately.
D is incorrect. The applicability of aggregation is based on the relationship of "holding or controlling" the positions, not on whether the position size exceeds a certain threshold.


### 温习册出处

当持仓量是为另一人持有或控制
有关须申报的持仓量和持仓上限的规则适用于由任何人士持有或控制的所有相关持仓量，意思即所有属《合约持仓量及申报规则》规定为同一类别的相关期货或股票期权合约的持仓量须合并计算，以确立某申报或持仓上限是否适用。就此而言，如任何人士可独立于主事人（即持仓量的拥有人）而行使酌情权买卖或出售期货或股票期权持仓量，则该名人士将被视为控制该等相关期货或股票期权合约。
另一方面，如只是因企业架构（例如母公司－附属公司关系）而获得控制权，而并无发出日常指示，母公司将不会被视为控制其附属公司或联属公司所拥有的任何相关期货或股票期权合约。

在判断一笔资金是否属于《客户款项规则》的管辖范围时，需要综合考虑多个因素。下列哪些情况下的客户款项，明确被排除在该规则的适用范围之外？
I. 由持牌法团的伦敦办事处，为进行证券交易而在当地收取并存放于伦敦银行账户的客户资金。
II. 持牌法团在香港收取客户款项后，按照规则要求操作，将其汇往纽约的托管银行账户以满足海外投资结算需求。
III. 客户为支付交易佣金，直接将一笔款项转账至持牌法团在恒生银行开立的公司运营账户。
IV. 客户为进行证券交易，将一笔款项存入持牌法团在星展银行开立的、指定为客户信托账户的银行账户。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

When determining whether a sum of money falls within the jurisdiction of the Client Money Rules, multiple factors need to be considered. In which of the following situations is the client money explicitly excluded from the scope of application of these rules?
I. Client funds collected locally by the London office of a licensed corporation for securities trading and deposited in a London bank account.
II. Client money received by a licensed corporation in Hong Kong and later, in accordance with the rules, remitted to a custodian bank account in New York to meet overseas investment settlement needs.
III. A client directly transfers a sum of money to the licensed corporation&#039;s corporate operating account at Hang Seng Bank to pay trading commissions.
IV. A client deposits a sum of money into a bank account opened by the licensed corporation at DBS Bank, designated as a client trust account, for securities trading.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确，规则不适用于持牌法团在香港以外地方收取或持有，而仍留在香港以外地方的客户款项。在伦敦收取并存于伦敦的款项符合此排除条件。II正确，规则不适用于持牌法团曾于任何时间在香港收取或持有，但已按照规则转移往香港以外地方的客户款项。从香港汇往纽约并留在该地的款项符合此排除条件。III正确，客户将款项直接转账至持牌法团的“公司运营账户”，这通常被视为持牌法团自身的公司账户，而非代客户持有款项的信托或客户账户。更重要的是，规则不适用于“客户以本身名义存放于银行帐户的持牌法团客户款项”，但此处款项进入了持牌法团自身账户，性质更偏向于公司收入（佣金），而非代持的客户款项。严格来说，它可能从一开始就不完全符合“客户款项”的某些定义，但结合规则排除条款的精神，此类进入公司账户的款项通常不被视为规则所规范的“客户款项”。IV错误，客户为进行证券交易将款项存入持牌法团开立的“客户信托账户”，这是典型的、在香港收取并持有的客户款项，属于《客户款项规则》的核心适用范围，并未被排除。


### Explanation

I is correct. The rules do not apply to client money received or held by a licensed corporation outside Hong Kong, and which remains outside Hong Kong. Money collected and held in London meets this exclusion condition. II is correct. The rules do not apply to client money that was received or held in Hong Kong by the licensed corporation or its associated entity at any time but has been transferred outside Hong Kong in accordance with the rules. Money remitted from Hong Kong to New York and remaining there meets this exclusion condition. III is correct. The client directly transferring money to the licensed corporation&#039;s "corporate operating account" is typically viewed as the licensed corporation&#039;s own corporate account, not a trust or client account for holding client money. More importantly, the rules do not apply to "client money of a licensed corporation that is deposited by the client in a bank account in the client&#039;s own name." However, here the money entered the licensed corporation&#039;s own account, its nature leaning more towards corporate revenue (commissions) rather than client money held on behalf. Strictly speaking, it might not fully meet certain definitions of "client money" from the outset, but considering the spirit of the exclusion provisions, such money entering a corporate account is generally not regarded as "client money" regulated by these rules. IV is incorrect. A client depositing money into a "client trust account" opened by the licensed corporation for securities trading is a typical example of client money received and held in Hong Kong, falling within the core scope of the Client Money Rules and is not excluded.


### 温习册出处

《证券及期货（客户款项）规则》
《客户款项规则》由证监会根据《证券及期货条例》第 149 条制定，适用于下述的持牌法团的客户款项︰
(a) 在进行受规管活动的过程中，由该持牌法团或代该持牌法团收取或持有；或
(b) 就该受规管活动的进行，由该持牌法团的有联系实体或代该实体收取或持有。
《客户款项规则》并不适用于：
(a) 持牌法团或其有联系实体在香港以外地方收取或持有，而仍留在香港以外地方的客户款项；
(b) 持牌法团或其有联系实体曾于任何时间在香港收取或持有，但已按照《客户款项规则》转移往香港以外地方的客户款项；或
(c) 客户以本身名义存放于银行帐户的持牌法团客户款项。

《风险管理指引》已于 2024 年 2 月 25 日生效。期货经纪行可于 12 个月的过渡期内采取临时措施，以符合将客户风险限额和压力测试纳入其系统和程序的目标。

## 第21题 (ID: 190905)

### 中文题目

一位申请成为持牌代表的个人拥有出色的学历和相关工作经验，但他最近因个人投资失败而负债累累，可能面临破产。根据“适当人选”的规定，监管机构可能会因此关注他的哪个方面？
A. 仅关注他的学历和经验，因为这与他的工作能力直接相关。
B. 关注他的信誉、品格和可靠程度，因为财务困境可能影响其判断力和可靠性。
C. 只关注他是否有能力诚实地进行活动，财务状况与此无关。
D. 财务状况不属于“适当人选”的评估范畴，因此不会考虑。


### English

An individual applying to become a licensed representative has excellent academic qualifications and relevant work experience, but has recently become heavily in debt due to personal investment failures and may face bankruptcy. According to the "fit and proper" requirements, which aspect of his profile might the regulator be concerned about as a result?
A. Only concerned with his academic qualifications and experience,as these are directly related to his work capability.
B. Concerned with his reputation,character,and reliability,as financial difficulties may affect his judgment and trustworthiness.
C. Only concerned with whether he can carry on the activity honestly; financial status is irrelevant to this.
D. Financial status is not within the scope of the "fit and proper" assessment,so it will not be considered.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，虽然学历和经验是重要方面，但严重的个人财务问题也属于“适当人选”的评估范畴，因为它关联到其信誉、可靠性和在财政方面的稳健性。
B正确，监管机构会评估有关人士的信誉、品格、可靠程度及在财政方面的稳健性。严重的个人财务困境可能被视为影响其可靠性或财政稳健性的负面因素，从而引发监管关注。
C错误，“诚实”是重要方面，但“适当人选”的评估是全面的，包括财务状况。
D错误，有关人士的财政状况或偿付能力以及财政方面的稳健性明确属于评估范畴。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. Although academic qualifications and experience are important aspects, serious personal financial problems also fall within the scope of the "fit and proper" assessment, as they relate to the individual&#039;s reputation, reliability, and financial soundness.
B is correct. The regulator will assess the person&#039;s reputation, character, reliability, and financial soundness. Serious personal financial difficulties may be viewed as a negative factor affecting their reliability or financial soundness, thereby attracting regulatory concern.
C is incorrect. "Honesty" is an important aspect, but the "fit and proper" assessment is comprehensive and includes financial status.
D is incorrect. The person&#039;s financial status or solvency, as well as their financial soundness, are explicitly within the scope of the assessment.


### 温习册出处

适当人选
适当人选规定分为四个主要类别（《证券及期货条例》）：
(a) 有关人士的财政状况或偿付能力；
(b) 有关人士的学历或其他资历或经验（在这方面的考虑会顾及该人将会执行的职能的性质）；
(c) 有关人士是否有能力称职地、诚实地且公正地进行有关受规管活动；及
(d) 有关人士的信誉、品格、可靠程度及在财政方面的稳健性。
对法团（认可财务机构除外）进行评估时，相同的规定适用于该法团的任何高级人员。

## 第22题 (ID: 190852)

### 中文题目

某资管公司在客户协议中概括性授权可接受经纪提供的“服务”，未明确具体内容。之后，公司从经纪处获得免费研究报告，但未将这一具体优惠的细节每年告知客户。这种做法主要违反了什么规定？
A. 非金钱利益必须对客户有利
B. 非金钱利益的具体详情必须每年向客户披露
C. 经纪佣金比率不得过高
D. 交易必须符合最佳执行条件


### English

An asset management firm obtained a general authorization in the client agreement to accept “services” from brokers without specifying details. Later, the firm received free research reports from a broker but did not annually inform clients of the specifics of this benefit. Which requirement does this practice primarily violate?
A. Non-monetary benefits must be beneficial to clients
B. Specific details of non-monetary benefits must be disclosed to clients annually
C. Brokerage commission rates must not be excessive
D. Trades must comply with best execution


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，虽然“对客户有利”是前提，但本题的核心违规是未按要求进行具体的年度披露。
B正确，收取非金钱利益后，必须将其具体详情（如免费报告）向客户披露，且至少每年一次。概括性授权和未做年度披露均不符合要求。
C错误，该规定涉及佣金水平，与本题中的非金钱利益形式无关。
D错误，该规定涉及交易执行质量，与利益披露程序无关。


### Explanation

A incorrect, while being “beneficial to clients” is a prerequisite, the core violation here is the failure to provide specific annual disclosure as required.
B correct, upon receiving non-monetary benefits, the specific details (e.g., free research reports) must be disclosed to clients, at least annually. General authorization and the lack of annual disclosure do not comply.
C incorrect, this requirement concerns commission levels and is not relevant to the form of non-monetary benefit in this question.
D incorrect, this requirement concerns the quality of trade execution and is not related to the benefit disclosure procedure.


### 温习册出处

回佣、非金钱利益和关连交易
行使投资酌情权代表客户行事及向经纪指示客户交易业务的持牌人或注册人，可收取经纪的非金钱利益（即物品或服务），前提为：
(a) 该等物品或服务明显地对持牌人或注册人的客户有利，证监会已就此提供一份其通常允许及其不允许的物品及服务的清单；
(b) 交易的执行符合最佳执行条件的原则；
(c) 经纪佣金比率不高于一般提供全面服务的经纪所收取的佣金比率；
(d) 有关客户已用书面方式同意收取有关物品或服务的做法，有关同意可以在客户协议或其他投资管理协议上载列；及
(e) 持牌人或注册人收取非金钱利益的做法已向客户作出披露，而披露内容须包括所收取的有关物品及服务的详情，并须最少每年一次向客户提供有关内容。

## 第23题 (ID: 190842)

某持牌法团的一名交易员因涉嫌操纵市场被廉政公署调查。该法团管理层在得知此事后，认为事件仍在调查中，尚未定罪，且不涉及公司系统问题，因此决定暂不向证监会汇报。这种做法是否符合规定？
A. 符合，因为事件仍在调查且未涉及公司系统
B. 不符合，因为雇员涉嫌严重违反法规，须立即向证监会汇报
C. 符合，因为调查机构是廉政公署而非证监会
D. 不符合，因为只有大股东问题才需汇报


### English

A trader at a licensed corporation was investigated by the ICAC for suspected market manipulation. Upon learning of this, the corporation&#039;s management decided not to report it to the SFC immediately, reasoning that the matter was still under investigation, no conviction had been made, and it did not involve the firm&#039;s systems. Does this practice comply with the rules?
A. Yes,because the matter is still under investigation and does not involve the firm&#039;s systems.
B. No,because an employee is suspected of seriously violating regulations,which requires immediate reporting to the SFC.
C. Yes,because the investigating body is the ICAC,not the SFC.
D. No,because only matters concerning major shareholders require reporting.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，事件已涉及“雇员涉嫌严重地违反任何法例”，属于须立即汇报的情况，与是否定罪或是否涉及系统问题无关。

C错误，无论调查由哪个机构进行，涉嫌严重违规的事实本身触发了向证监会的汇报义务。
D错误，需汇报的情况有多种，雇员严重违规是其中明确的一种。


### Explanation

A incorrect, the incident involves "an employee being suspected of seriously violating any law," which is a reportable situation requiring immediate reporting, regardless of whether a conviction has been made or if it involves the firm&#039;s systems.
B correct, the rules require immediate reporting to the SFC upon discovering that an employee is suspected of seriously violating any law or SFC rules, among other situations.
C incorrect, regardless of which authority is conducting the investigation, the fact of suspected serious violation itself triggers the reporting obligation to the SFC.
D incorrect, there are multiple reportable situations; serious violation by an employee is explicitly one of them.

## 第24题 (ID: 190954)

### 中文题目

某持牌法团的人力资源总监正在为新入职的持牌代表制定第一年的培训计划。为确保该计划符合监管机构对持牌人维持专业水平的基本期望，该总监应主要参考以下哪一份文件来设定培训的框架与方向？
A. 《证券及期货事务监察委员会持牌人或注册人操守准则》
B. 《发牌手册》
C. 《胜任能力的指引》
D. 《持续培训的指引》


### English

The Human Resources Director of a licensed corporation is developing a training plan for the first year of newly hired licensed representatives. To ensure the plan aligns with the regulator&#039;s basic expectations for licensed persons to maintain professional standards, which of the following documents should the director primarily refer to for setting the framework and direction of the training?
A. Code of Conduct for Persons Licensed by or Registered with the Securities and Futures Commission
B. Licensing Handbook
C. Guidelines on Competence
D. Guidelines on Continuous Professional Training


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，《操守准则》主要规范职业道德和行为操守，并非专门针对持续专业培训的要求。
B错误，《发牌手册》侧重于牌照申请和注册的初始程序与规定。
C错误，《胜任能力的指引》主要载列申请时或成为持牌人初期应达到的胜任能力要求，而非针对“持续”培训。
D正确，因为《持续培训的指引》的目的正是提供证监会对持牌人或注册人为继续成为适当人选而进行持续专业培训的要求的一般指引。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The Code of Conduct mainly regulates professional ethics and conduct, not specifically the requirements for continuous professional training.
B is incorrect. The Licensing Handbook focuses on the initial procedures and regulations for license application and registration.
C is incorrect. The Guidelines on Competence primarily list the competence requirements to be met at the time of application or upon initial becoming licensed, not for "continuous" training.
D is correct because the purpose of the Guidelines on Continuous Professional Training is precisely to provide general guidance on the SFC&#039;s requirements for licensed or registered persons to undertake continuous professional training to remain fit and proper.


### 温习册出处

持牌代表处理客户指令时，因内部操作原因，考虑延迟处理客户的限价单。在向客户推介新产品时，提议赠送纪念品。此外，未对客户的电话交易指令录音。以下哪些判断是正确的？
I. 延迟处理限价单的行为可能不合规。
II. 赠送产品纪念品的行为可能不合规。
III. 未能以市场最优条件执行交易可能不合规。
IV. 未对电话指令录音可能不合规。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

A licensed representative considers delaying the processing of a client&#039;s limit order due to internal operational reasons. When recommending a new product to the client, the representative proposes offering a commemorative gift. Additionally, the client&#039;s trading instruction given via telephone is not recorded. Which of the following assessments are correct?
I. The act of delaying the limit order may be non-compliant.
II. The act of offering a product commemorative gift may be non-compliant.
III. Failing to execute the trade at the best market terms may be non-compliant.
IV. Not recording the telephone instruction may be non-compliant.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确，因为客户的交易指示必须尽快执行，不能因内部操作便利而被推迟或暂缓。
II正确，因为在向客户推销具体投资产品时，不应提供任何赠品（费用折扣除外），以保持建议的客观性并防止不当诱导。
III错误，因为情景描述中并未涉及交易执行条件的问题，没有信息表明代表在执行交易时没有寻求最佳可得条件。
IV正确，因为通过电话接收客户交易指示时，应当使用录音系统进行记录并保存至少一段规定的时间，这是确保指令准确、完整并可被追溯的关键程序。


### Explanation

I correct, because client trading instructions must be executed promptly and should not be postponed or suspended for internal operational convenience.
II correct, because when promoting a specific investment product to a client, no gifts should be offered (except for fee discounts) to maintain the objectivity of advice and prevent improper inducement.
III incorrect, because the scenario description does not involve the terms of trade execution; there is no information indicating that the representative failed to seek the best available conditions when executing the trade.
IV correct, because when receiving client trading instructions via telephone, a recording system should be used to document and retain the instructions for at least a specified period, which is a key procedure to ensure the accuracy, completeness, and traceability of the instructions.


### 温习册出处

有关的电话录音（作为持牌人或注册人的纪录的一部分）至少 6 个月。持牌人
或注册人应禁止其职员在交易场地、盘房、收取交易指示的通常营业地点或通
常经营业务的地点内使用流动电话收取客户交易指示，并应制定书面政策解释
和执行这项禁制。但若以流动电话于以上所提及的禁止地点以外的地方接受交易指示，便应立即致电办事处的电话录音系统以记录收到交易指示的时间及有
关指示的详情。只有在不能接驳到持牌人或注册人的电话录音系统的情况下才
应使用其他方式（例如用人手以书面方式）将收到交易指示的时间及有关指示

## 第34题 (ID: 190869)

### 中文题目

“永安期货”在周五收市后的风险检查中，发现其有联系实体在处理某客户证券时，操作流程存在瑕疵，这可能构成了对《客户证券规则》的违反。关于永安期货的报告义务，以下说法正确的是？
A. 由于发现时间已是周五收市后，可以顺延至下周一（即下一个营业日）再向证监会报告。
B. 必须在周六（即下一个日历日）结束前，设法向证监会提交书面通知。
C. 因为违规主体是“有联系实体”而非中介人本身，所以永安期货无需承担报告责任。
D. 可以先联系该有联系实体要求其整改，若下周内整改完毕，则无需再行报告。


### English

Wing On Futures, during a post-market risk check on Friday, discovered that its associated entity had procedural flaws in handling a client&#039;s securities, which may constitute a breach of the Client Securities Rules. Regarding Wing On Futures&#039; reporting obligation, which of the following statements is correct?
A. Since the issue was discovered after market close on Friday,it is permissible to postpone reporting to the Securities and Futures Commission until next Monday (i.e.,the next business day).
B. A written notification must be submitted to the Securities and Futures Commission by the end of Saturday (i.e.,the next calendar day).
C. Since the entity in violation is an "affiliated entity" and not the intermediary itself,Wing On Futures is not obligated to assume reporting responsibility.
D. The intermediary may first contact the affiliated entity to request rectification. If the issue is resolved within the following week,no further reporting is required.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A正确，报告时限的计算单位是“营业日”。周五收市后发现违规，下一个营业日通常是下周一（除非周六是营业日）。因此，报告义务的履行可以顺延至下周一结束前。B错误，营业日通常指交易所的开市日，周六一般不是营业日。报告时限是按“营业日”计算，而非“日历日”。C错误，中介人对其有联系实体在处理客户资产方面的合规性负有监督和管理责任。当察觉有联系实体违反《客户证券规则》时，中介人自身的报告责任已被触发。D错误，内部整改行动不能免除或替代向监管机构进行即时书面报告的法定责任。报告义务独立于任何补救措施。


### Explanation

A is correct. The unit for calculating the reporting time limit is "business day." If a violation is discovered after market close on Friday, the next business day is typically next Monday (unless Saturday is a business day). Therefore, the fulfillment of the reporting obligation can be postponed until the end of next Monday. B is incorrect. A business day typically refers to an exchange trading day; Saturday is generally not a business day. The reporting time limit is calculated in "business days," not "calendar days." C is incorrect. The intermediary has supervisory and management responsibilities for the compliance of its associated entities regarding the handling of client assets. When becoming aware that an associated entity has breached the Client Securities Rules, the intermediary&#039;s own reporting duty is triggered. D is incorrect. Internal rectification actions cannot exempt or substitute for the statutory duty of providing immediate written notice to the regulator. The reporting obligation is independent of any remedial measures.

由于违反《客户证券规则》的行为可能会严重损害对投资者的保障，因此，中介人有责任于察觉若干《客户证券规则》的指明条文未获遵守后的一个营业日内向证监会发出书面通知。

### 中文题目

关于中介人在偿付能力方面的禁止性条件，以下哪些人士或实体将被认为不符合要求？

"Regarding the prohibitive conditions concerning the solvency of intermediaries, which of the following persons or entities would be considered not to meet the requirements?
I. An individual who is the subject of bankruptcy proceedings.
II. A company that has failed to pay a debt finally adjudicated by a court.
III. An individual who has outstanding debts, but these debts have not been adjudicated by a court.
IV. A person who was previously bankrupt but has been lawfully discharged from bankruptcy for more than five years."
A. I,II
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I 正确，中介人不得为遭受破产诉讼的人士。正在进行破产诉讼即属于此列。

IV is incorrect. The provision prohibits the current state of "being the subject of bankruptcy proceedings." If a bankruptcy order has been discharged and the person is no longer subject to proceedings, whether this poses an obstacle would be assessed based on other factors (such as whether they are considered fit and proper). However, the mere fact of "having been bankrupt in the past but discharged for over five years" does not necessarily directly violate this specific provision concerning the person&#039;s current status. The provision targets the current state.

## 第37题 (ID: 190610)

### 中文题目

某公司董事因参与重大项目而获悉内幕消息。在此期间，他按照预先设定的员工股权计划行权买入公司股票。在被质疑可能涉及内幕交易时，他提出其买入行为是基于早已获得的行权权利。这最可能对应以下哪项免责理由？

B. I,II

### English

A company director learned inside information while involved in a major project. During this period, he exercised pre-existing employee stock options to purchase company shares. When questioned about potential insider dealing, he argued that the purchase was based on a right obtained earlier. Which defense does this most closely correspond to?
I. Exercising an existing right obtained before knowing the inside information.
II. The company had effective functional and information segregation mechanisms.
III. The purpose of the transaction was not to profit from the inside information.
IV. The counterparty knew he was a connected person of the company.
A. I
B. I,II
C. I,III
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确。行权购买是基于知悉消息前已获得的认购权利，这属于一项有效辩护理由。

IV incorrect. This defense applies to specific transactions with a knowledgeable counterparty, whereas this case involves exercising options in the open market without a designated counterparty.

内幕交易指控的免责辩护
《证券及期货条例》规定可能作出的若干辩护如下：
(a) 职能分隔制度 － 倘若法团一名或多名董事或雇员掌握内幕消息而该法团
进行交易（或怂使进行交易等），如该等实质（或怂使）参与交易的人士于
有关时间并无掌握内幕消息（即他们受有效的职能分隔制度分隔），则该法
团不会被视为从事内幕交易；
(b) 豁除目的 － 透过进行或怂使他人进行交易等而发生的内幕交易的目的并非在于利用内幕消息而获利或避免损失；
(c) 市场合约 － 有关交易为一项市场合约；
(d) 关连人士的交易 － 交易的另一方知道或理应知道交易对手为关连人士
（附注：这辩护只在该关连人士并没有怂使或促致交易的另一方进行该交易的情况下方为有效。）；及
(e) 行使现有权利 － 如在知道内幕消息前已获得认购或购买证券的权利，则因行使有关权利而进行的交易不会被视为内幕交易。
尽管证明内幕交易有时十分困难，但个人或公司的名誉会因涉及有关事件而受损。

## 第39题 (ID: 190628)

### 中文题目

某券商对所有客户采用相同的监控标准，未根据风险高低区别对待。结果一名高风险客户的异常交易未被发现。该券商在持续监察方面可能存在哪些问题？
I. 未根据客户风险水平调整监控程度。
II. 未确保客户活动与其已知情况相符。
III. 未对高风险客户加强审查。
IV. 未定期更新客户资料。
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


### English

A brokerage uses the same monitoring standards for all clients without differentiation based on risk levels. As a result, abnormal transactions by a high-risk client were not detected. What issues might the brokerage have in ongoing monitoring?
I. Did not adjust monitoring levels based on client risk levels.
II. Did not ensure client activities matched their known profile.
III. Did not enhance scrutiny for high-risk clients.
IV. Did not periodically update client information.
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确。应采用风险为本的方法，对高风险客户加强监控，统一标准不符合要求。
II正确。监控应确保客户交易与其业务和风险状况一致，未能发现异常表明不足。
III正确。高风险客户应接受更严格审查，未加强审查存在问题。

### Explanation

I correct. A risk-based approach should be used to intensify monitoring for high-risk clients. Uniform standards are non-compliant.
II correct. Monitoring should ensure client transactions align with their business and risk profile. Failure to detect discrepancies indicates a deficiency.
III correct. High-risk clients should undergo stricter scrutiny. Lack of enhanced review is an issue.
IV incorrect. The scenario does not mention updating client information, so this cannot be assessed.


### 温习册出处

持续监察
一旦已开立客户户口，须持续监察客户户口的活动，以便侦察异常或可疑活动。
这要求其持续监察与客户的业务关系，包括客户的活动与中介人对客户以及其
业务、风险状况及资金来源的认知相符。持续监察的程度应遵循风险为本的方
法，以增加对风险较高的客户的监控，并减少对风险较低的客户的监控。

## 第40题 (ID: 190608)

### 中文题目

交易员A在收市前几分钟，用多个账户大量买入某只股票，目的是让该股收盘价达到一个更高的整数位，以改善其投资组合的当日表现。这种交易不改变股票的实益所有权。这可能是什么行为？
A. 合法的收盘价优化
B. 操控价格
C. 内幕交易
D. 虚假交易


### English

Trader A, in the final minutes before the market close, uses multiple accounts to heavily buy a stock, aiming to push its closing price to a higher round number to improve his portfolio&#039;s daily performance. These trades involve no change in beneficial ownership. What might this be?
A. Legitimate closing price optimization
B. Price rigging
C. Insider dealing
D. False trading


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，通过无实益所有权转变的交易人为影响收盘价，并非合法优化。
B正确，订立不涉及实益所有权转变的交易，意图影响（此处为提高）证券收盘价，符合操控价格的定义。
C错误，未利用未公开信息。
D错误，虚假交易侧重制造交投活跃或价格的虚假表象，而本案行为是意图直接影响并锁定一个具体价格（收盘价），更直接地符合操控价格。


### Explanation

A incorrect. Artificially influencing the closing price through trades with no change in beneficial ownership is not legitimate optimization.
B correct. Entering into transactions with no change in beneficial ownership, intending to affect (here, increase) the price of a security, fits the definition of price rigging.
C incorrect. No undisclosed information is used.
D incorrect. False trading focuses on creating a false appearance of trading activity or price, whereas this act aims to directly influence and set a specific price (the closing price), more directly aligning with price rigging.


### 温习册出处

操控价格
如任何人涉及下列情况，则操控价格的行为即告发生：
(a) 订立当中不涉及实益拥有权转变的证券买卖而该交易具有维持、提高、降低、稳定证券价格或引致证券价格波动的效果；或
(b) 意图或罔顾后果地订立或履行可维持、提高、降低、稳定证券或期货合约价格或引致证券或期货合约价格波动的虚构或非真实的交易或手段。
上述交易包括：
(a) 于香港或其他地方进行的交易（就在认可市场或透过使用认可自动化交易服务买卖的证券或期货合约而言）；或
(b) 于香港进行的交易（就在海外市场买卖的证券或期货合约而言）。
附注：操控价格仅适用于在交易所或透过认可自动化交易服务进行的交易。
提供相关交易的合理理据，可作为失当行为或罪行的辩护。
