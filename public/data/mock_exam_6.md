## 第1题 (ID: 190934)

### 中文题目

一家在香港注册并受金管局规管的银行，计划开展证券交易（一种证监会规管活动）。关于其开展此项业务的合规路径，以下哪项是正确的？
A. 该银行必须向证监会申请成为“持牌法团”，并完全受证监会单一规管。
B. 该银行必须终止其金管局的许可，转而向证监会申请成为持牌法团。
C. 该银行须向证监会注册为“注册机构”，由其与金管局共同规管，但金管局作为前线监管机构。
D. 该银行只需获得其内部董事会的批准即可开展业务，因为银行已受金管局规管。


### English

A bank in Hong Kong that is regulated by the HKMA and plans to engage in securities trading (a regulated activity under the SFO) must be registered with the SFC. The HKMA acts as the front-line regulator for the day-to-day supervision of these activities.
A. The bank must apply to the SFC to become a "licensed corporation" and be solely regulated by the SFC.
B. The bank must terminate its authorization from the HKMA and apply to the SFC to become a licensed corporation.
C. The bank must register with the SFC as a "registered institution," and be jointly regulated by the SFC and HKMA,with the HKMA acting as the front-line regulator.
D. The bank only needs internal board approval to commence the business,as it is already regulated by the HKMA.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，获金管局许可及受其规管的认可财务机构从事证监会受规管活动时，是向证监会注册为“注册机构”，而非申请成为“持牌法团”。
B错误，认可财务机构无需终止其金管局的许可，它是在维持其金管局许可身份的同时，向证监会注册。
C正确，获金管局许可及受其规管并从事证监会受规管活动的认可财务机构，须向证监会注册为“注册机构”。注册机构由金管局及证监会共同规管，但以金管局作为前线监管机构。
D错误，开展证监会规管活动需要正式的注册或发牌，仅内部董事会批准是不够的。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. When an authorized financial institution authorized and regulated by the HKMA carries on SFC-regulated activities, it registers with the SFC as a "registered institution," rather than applying to become a "licensed corporation."
B is incorrect. An authorized financial institution does not need to terminate its HKMA authorization; it registers with the SFC while maintaining its status as an HKMA-authorized institution.
C is correct. An authorized financial institution that is authorized and regulated by the HKMA and carries on SFC-regulated activities must register with the SFC as a "registered institution." Registered institutions are jointly regulated by the HKMA and the SFC, with the HKMA acting as the front-line regulator.
D is incorrect. Engaging in SFC-regulated activities requires formal registration or licensing; internal board approval alone is insufficient.


### 温习册出处

《证券及期货条例》下的发牌及注册规定
《证券及期货条例》规定证监会负责执行发牌制度。有关制度规定涵盖13 种特定的受规管活动的单一牌照或注册。每张牌照或注册上均批注有该牌照准许从事作为业务的受规管活动，亦包括证监会可能施加的任何发牌或注册条件。
牌照分为两类：一类发予法团（“持牌法团”）及另一类发予个人（“持牌代表”）。一名代表通常仅隶属于一个法团，但证监会可核准其隶属于同一公司集团内的多间持牌法团。
就每类受规管活动而言，持牌法团必须具备至少两名负责人员。
获香港金融管理局（“金管局”）许可及受其规管并从事证监会受规管活动的认可财务机构（包括银行），须向证监会注册为“注册机构”，而基于认可财务机构的特点，注册机构成为特别的类别。注册机构由金管局及证监会共同规管，但以金管局作为前线监管机构。金管局在规管注册机构时，会采用所有证监会的规管准则，包括适当人选及业务操守等方面的准则（资本充足水平、处理客户款项及审计规定除外）。由于“发牌”一词将用于直接受证监会规管的所有业务及人士，故所有对注册的提述一般适用于本类别。
注册机构须遵守《银行业条例》中有关委任最少两名主管人员负责直接监督其受规管活动的条文。
以下为与本章有关的规则、手册及指引：
(a) 《证券及期货（发牌及注册）（资料）规则》（“《资料规则》”）订明须载入申请文件及周年申报表并提交证监会的资料；
(b) 《发牌手册》详述发牌或注册的规定以及申请牌照和注册的程序；
(c) 《适当人选的指引》阐释适当人选的准则，并载列证监会在考虑某人是否为获发牌或注册的适当人选时所考虑的各种事项；
(d) 《胜任能力的指引》载列注册人或持牌人及拟获发牌或注册的人士的胜任能力的要求；及
(e) 《持续培训的指引》旨在提供证监会对代表继续作为适当人选而进行持续培训的要求的一般指引。

## 第2题 (ID: 190838)

### 中文题目

一位客户致电其持牌投资顾问，授权其朋友张先生在未来一周内代为操作其证券账户。持牌顾问接受了该电话授权，验证了张先生的身份证件并留存了复印件，但未获得客户就该授权出具的书面文件。以下哪些判断正确？
I. 持牌顾问采纳了第三方指示，不合规。
II. 持牌顾问验证并记录了第三方身份，合规。
III. 持牌顾问获得了客户的授权，合规。
IV. 持牌顾问未取得客户书面授权，不合规。
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


### English

A client called his licensed investment advisor to authorize his friend, Mr. Zhang, to operate his securities account on his behalf for the upcoming week. The licensed advisor accepted the telephone authorization, verified Mr. Zhang’s identification document and kept a copy, but did not obtain a written document from the client for this authorization. Which of the following assessments are correct?
I. The licensed advisor’s acceptance of instructions from a third party is non-compliant.
II. The licensed advisor’s verification and recording of the third party’s identity is compliant.
III. The licensed advisor’s obtaining of the client’s authorization is compliant.
IV. The licensed advisor’s failure to obtain the client’s written authorization is non-compliant.
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I错误，持牌顾问可以接纳第三方操作客户账户，前提是满足特定条件，因此采纳行为本身并非不合规。
II正确，持牌顾问验证了张先生的身份证件并留存复印件，这符合确立并保存第三方身份资料的要求。
III错误，尽管客户通过电话进行了授权，但规定要求取得的是“书面授权”，电话授权不符合此形式要求。
IV正确，因为接纳第三方操作客户账户，必须取得客户的“书面授权”。未取得书面文件不合规。


### Explanation

I incorrect, a licensed advisor may accept instructions from a third party to operate a client&#039;s account, provided specific conditions are met. Therefore, the act of acceptance itself is not non-compliant.
II correct, the licensed advisor verified Mr. Zhang’s ID and kept a copy. This complies with the requirement to establish and maintain a record of the third party&#039;s identity.
III incorrect, although the client authorized via telephone, the requirement is to obtain the client’s “written authorization.” Telephone authorization does not meet this formal requirement.
IV correct, because accepting a third party to operate a client’s account requires obtaining the client’s “written authorization.” Failure to obtain a written document is non-compliant.


### 温习册出处

以第三方授权操作客户的帐户
持牌人或注册人接纳第三方就一名客户的帐户而发出的指示前，《操守准则》规
定持牌人或注册人及其职员须采取合理步骤，以确立第三方的真实和全部身份，
并保存第三方身份资料的纪录。持牌人或注册人应就第三方操作客户的帐户取
得客户的书面授权。

## 第3题 (ID: 190502)

### 中文题目

在评估一项场外衍生工具交易是否产生结算责任时，需要考虑“订立交易的时间及地点”。这主要反映了以下哪项原则？
A. 结算责任具有全球普遍性，与时间地点无关。
B. 结算责任可能因司法管辖区或法规生效日期的不同而有所差异。
C. 交易时间仅影响结算费用的计算。
D. 交易地点仅决定适用的合同法，不影响监管结算责任。


### English

When assessing whether an OTC derivatives transaction gives rise to a clearing obligation, the "time and place of entering into the transaction" need to be considered. This primarily reflects which of the following principles?
A. The clearing obligation is globally universal and independent of time and place.
B. The clearing obligation may vary depending on the jurisdiction or the effective date of regulations.
C. The time of transaction only affects the calculation of clearing fees.
D. The place of transaction only determines the applicable contract law and does not affect regulatory clearing obligations.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误。结算责任可能因法规在不同时间地点生效而不同。
B正确。“订立交易的时间及地点”是产生结算责任的因素之一，这反映了责任可能因司法管辖区法规不同或法规在不同时间生效而有所差异。
C错误。交易时间作为因素之一，其影响在于是否触发结算责任，而非仅影响费用计算。
D错误。交易地点可能影响适用的监管法规，从而影响结算责任。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The clearing obligation may differ as regulations come into effect in different jurisdictions and at different times.
B is correct. The "time and place of entering into the transaction" is one of the factors giving rise to a clearing obligation. This reflects that the obligation may vary depending on jurisdictional regulations or when regulations take effect.
C is incorrect. As one of the factors, the time of transaction affects whether the clearing obligation is triggered, not merely fee calculation.
D is incorrect. The place of transaction may affect which regulatory regime applies, thus impacting the clearing obligation.


### 温习册出处

适用范围
根据《场外衍生工具结算规则》，会产生须透过指定中央对手方结算场外衍生工具交易的责任的因素如下：
(a) 交易的产品类型；
(b) 属交易对手方的人士的性质；
(c) 交易对手方是否已达到“结算门槛”；
(d) 订立交易的时间及地点；
(e) 豁免是否适用；及
(f) 责任是否可透过替代遵守制度被视为已获遵守。
上述各项因素于下文阐释。
值得注意的是，即使某项交易并无负有结算责任，该项交易无论如何仍可能负有汇报及备存纪录责任。

## 第4题 (ID: 190637)

### 中文题目

某金融机构在客户筛查时，发现一名客户与官方公布的恐怖分子名单中的人员姓名高度相似。该机构未采取进一步核查或报告，仍继续为其提供资金转账服务。后经警方调查，确认该客户确与恐怖活动有关联。该机构的行为可能涉及哪些违规？
I. 可能涉及向恐怖分子或有关联者提供金融服务。
II. 未对可疑客户采取冻结财产的措施。
III. 未将可疑情况向指定人员披露。
IV. 未配合警方调查并提供相关资料。
A. I,II,III
B. I,III,IV
C. II,III,IV
D. I,II,IV


### English

During customer screening, a financial institution found that a client&#039;s name closely matched that of a person on the officially published terrorist list. The institution did not conduct further verification or reporting and continued to provide fund transfer services. Subsequent police investigation confirmed the client was indeed linked to terrorist activities. Which violations may the institution&#039;s actions involve?
I. May involve providing financial services to terrorists or associated persons.
II. Failed to take measures to freeze the client&#039;s assets.
III. Did not disclose the suspicious situation to designated personnel.
IV. Did not cooperate with police investigation by providing relevant information.
A. I,II,III
B. I,III,IV
C. II,III,IV
D. I,II,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确。为被确认为与恐怖分子有关联的客户持续提供转账服务，可能构成向恐怖分子或有关联者提供金融服务。
II错误。财产冻结措施通常由执法机关依法实施，金融机构在未接到明确指令或法律文书前，无权主动冻结客户财产。情景中未提及该机构收到此类指令。
III正确。当发现客户可能涉及恐怖活动时，有义务向指定人员报告可疑情况，未报告可能构成违规。
IV正确。该条例授权调查机关强制要求提供资料，未配合警方调查并提供资料可能违反相关规定。


### Explanation

I correct. Continuing to provide transfer services to a client confirmed to be linked to terrorists may constitute providing financial services to terrorists or associated persons.
II incorrect. Freezing assets is typically enforced by law enforcement authorities based on legal orders. Financial institutions cannot proactively freeze client assets without receiving specific instructions or legal documents, which were not mentioned in the scenario.
III correct. When a client is suspected of involvement in terrorist activities, there is an obligation to report the suspicious situation to designated personnel. Failure to do so may be non-compliant.
IV correct. The relevant regulations authorize investigating authorities to compel the provision of information. Failure to cooperate with police investigations and provide information may violate these requirements.


### 温习册出处

《有组织及严重罪行条例》
《有组织及严重罪行条例》授权警方取得法令，强制任何人士提供与调查有组
织罪行相关的资料或材料，并进行搜查。作披露及接受搜查的规定凌驾任何保
密责任之上。就有关财产的处理、披露、豁免以及发出限制令而言，《有组织及
严重罪行条例》亦订有与《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》类似的条文。
《联合国反恐条例》
《联合国反恐条例》旨在实施相关的联合国安全理事会决议（“安理会决议”）中
关于阻止向恐怖主义行动提供资金的决定。宪报会不时刊登恐怖分子及与恐怖
分子有联系者的名单。《联合国反恐条例》有以下规定：
(a) 任何人士如向恐怖分子或与恐怖分子有联系者提供财产或金融服务，或为恐怖分子或与恐怖分子有联系者筹集财产或寻求金融服务，即属犯罪；
(b) 容许将恐怖分子的财产冻结及／或充公；及
(c) 要求知悉或怀疑财产属于恐怖分子的人士，向获授权人员作出披露，违者即属犯罪。
《联合国反恐条例》订有与前述《贩毒（追讨得益）条例》及《有组织及严重罪行条例》中有关披露及豁免的条文类似的条文。

## 第5题 (ID: 190938)

### 中文题目

为确保市场稳定，防止个别参与者过度集中风险，香港交易所对某些衍生产品合约设定了持仓上限。营运科市场监察职能需要持续监控并处理大额持仓申报。这项监控工作属于其哪项具体职责？
A. 监察交易所参与者在现货及衍生产品市场进行的交易活动。
B. 监察中华通交易活动及其遵守规则与规例。
C. 监察衍生产品合约的持仓上限和大额未平仓合约申报的情况。
D. 监察庄家申请。


### English

To ensure market stability and prevent excessive risk concentration by individual participants, HKEX sets position limits for certain derivatives contracts. The market surveillance function of the Operations Division needs to continuously monitor and handle large position reports. Which specific responsibility does this monitoring work belong to?
A. Monitoring the trading activities conducted by exchange participants in the cash and derivatives markets.
B. Monitoring Stock Connect trading activities and their compliance with rules and regulations.
C. Monitoring position limits for derivatives contracts and the reporting of large open positions.
D. Monitoring market maker applications.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，此项职责是对“交易活动”的监察，侧重于交易行为本身；而题干描述的是对“持仓”结果（即未平仓合约）的监控，属于风险管理范畴。
B错误，此项职责专门针对“中华通”这一特定交易机制，与对所有衍生产品合约持仓上限的普遍性监控不同。
C正确，因为条文明确将“监察衍生产品合约的持仓上限和大额未平仓合约申报的情况”列为市场监察职能的一项独立职责。
D错误，庄家申请监察是关于市场参与者申请成为做市商的资格审核，与监控现有合约的持仓量无关。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. This responsibility involves monitoring trading activities, focusing on the trading behavior itself. The question describes monitoring the position outcome, which falls under risk management.
B is incorrect. This responsibility is specific to the Stock Connect trading mechanism and differs from the general monitoring of position limits for all derivatives contracts.
C is correct because the text explicitly lists monitoring position limits for derivatives contracts and the reporting of large open positions as a distinct responsibility of the market surveillance function.
D is incorrect. The surveillance of market maker applications involves the qualification review of market participants applying to become market makers and is unrelated to monitoring the open interest of existing contracts.

(c) 衍生产品合约的持仓上限和大额未平仓合约申报的情况；及

## 第6题 (ID: 190947)

### 中文题目

证监会与香港金融管理局（金管局）定期举行联席会议，共享关于跨市场风险及个别机构合规状况的信息，并协调监管行动。这种合作机制主要是为了履行证监会的哪一项职能？
A. 维持及提高公众对证券期货业的信心。
B. 促进、鼓励及以强制方式确保进行受规管活动的人士必须操守正当、力足胜任及廉洁自持。
C. 与其他监管机构紧密合作及向其提供协助。
D. 促进投资者教育，提高投资者对金融服务的了解。


### English

The SFC and the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) hold regular joint meetings to share information on cross-market risks and the compliance status of individual institutions and to coordinate regulatory actions. This cooperation mechanism is primarily intended to fulfill which of the SFC&#039;s functions?
A. To maintain and enhance public confidence in the securities and futures industry.
B. To promote,encourage,and ensure by mandatory means that persons carrying on regulated activities act with integrity,competence,and in an upright manner.
C. To cooperate closely with and provide assistance to other regulatory authorities.
D. To promote investor education and enhance investors&#039; understanding of financial services.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误。维持公众信心是监管的最终目标之一，但题干具体描述的是与其他监管机构（金管局）进行信息共享和行动协调的“合作机制”，这直接对应的是“合作”职能本身。
B错误。该职能针对的是受规管活动人士的个人操守和胜任能力，与监管机构之间的跨部门协作无直接关系。
C正确。与金管局这类其他监管机构举行会议、共享信息、协调行动，是“与其他监管机构紧密合作及向其提供协助”这一职能的典型体现。
D错误。该职能针对的是面向投资者的教育工作，而非监管机构之间的协作。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. Maintaining public confidence is one of the ultimate goals of regulation, but the question specifically describes a "cooperation mechanism" involving information sharing and action coordination with another regulator (HKMA). This directly corresponds to the "cooperation" function itself.
B is incorrect. This function targets the personal conduct and competence of persons carrying on regulated activities and is not directly related to inter-agency collaboration between regulators.
C is correct. Holding meetings, sharing information, and coordinating actions with another regulatory authority like the HKMA is a typical manifestation of the function "to cooperate closely with and provide assistance to other regulatory authorities."
D is incorrect. This function targets educational work for investors, not collaboration between regulators.


### 温习册出处

证券及期货事务监察委员会（“证监会”）
职能及权力

(a) 采取适当措施以维持及促进证券及期货业的公平、效率、竞争、透明度及秩序；

### 中文题目

IV. 将该批债券继续持有作为抵押品，但不能主动处置，必须等待吴女士后续追加资金。

B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


### English

Client Ms. Wu provided bonds to "CITIC Futures" as collateral for her stock index futures trading. Both parties have entered into a written agreement, and the agreement grants CITIC Futures an appropriate lien. Later, Ms. Wu defaults. In this situation, which of the following actions may CITIC Futures take in accordance with the Client Securities Rules?
I. Set off Ms. Wu&#039;s owed debt with this batch of bond collateral.
II. Without going through other procedures, directly transfer this batch of bonds to the company&#039;s finance department as its own assets.
III. Realize (sell) this batch of bonds to repay Ms. Wu&#039;s relevant debts arising from futures trading.
IV. Continue holding this batch of bonds as collateral but cannot actively dispose of them; must wait for Ms. Wu to subsequently inject additional funds.
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,IV
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确，当中介人依据一份授予其适当留置权的有效合约接纳了证券抵押品，并在客户失责时，有权以该抵押品抵销客户所欠债务。II错误，将客户抵押品直接划转为中介人自有资产，是严重的违规行为，违反了作为受托人持有客户资产的基本义务和资产隔离规定。允许的行为是抵销债务并变现偿债，而非侵占资产。III正确，客户失责时，中介人有权将作为抵押品的证券变现，所得款项用于偿还客户的相关债务。IV错误，在客户失责且中介人拥有适当留置权的情况下，中介人有权主动处置抵押品以清偿债务，而不仅仅是被动持有。等待客户追加资金并非其唯一或强制选项。

In the event of client default and where the intermediary holds an appropriate lien, the intermediary has the right to actively dispose of the collateral to settle the debt, not merely to hold it passively. 当相同资产的所有关联产品的全部 16 种情况全部配对后，则 PRiME 将从矩阵数目中查找投资组合的最大正数总值，并将其作为投资组合（单一资产）的开仓按金要求。

### 温习册出处

《证券及期货条例》不适用于专业投资者的条文
所有类别的专业投资者不受下列《证券及期货条例》的条文所规限。
(a) 《证券及期货条例》有关未获邀约的造访的条文（即是可以擅自造访他们）；及
(b) 《证券及期货条例》订明的关于中介人或代表提出的要约的规定。
附注：专业投资者的定义亦与若干发牌及产品认可有关。
