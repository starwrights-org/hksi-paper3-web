## 第1题 (ID: 190868)

### 中文题目

“四海期货”被查出系统性违反《客户证券规则》中关于处理客户证券抵押品的限制性规定，且公司未能提供任何合理辩解。根据规定，此类“无合理辩解”的违规，若循公诉程序定罪，最高可能面临何种刑罚？
A. 可处第6级罚款。
B. 最高可处罚款20万港元及监禁两年。
C. 最高可处罚款100万港元及监禁7年。
D. 仅由证监会采取纪律处分，不涉及刑事监禁。


### English

Four Seas Futures was found to have systematically violated the restrictive provisions regarding the handling of client securities collateral in the Client Securities Rules and failed to provide any reasonable excuse. According to the provisions, for such a violation "without reasonable excuse," if convicted on indictment, what is the maximum possible penalty?
A. Liable to a fine at level 6.
B. Liable to a maximum fine of HK$200,000 and imprisonment for 2 years.
C. Liable to a maximum fine of HK$1 million and imprisonment for 7 years.
D. Only disciplinary action by the SFC,not involving criminal imprisonment.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，第6级罚款是针对“意图诈骗”而违反某些特定条款（如常设授权规定）的处罚，或是“无合理辩解”违规在循简易程序定罪时的罚款选项之一。本题问的是“无合理辩解”违规且循公诉程序定罪的最高刑罚。B正确，对于“无合理辩解”而违反《客户证券规则》第5或10(1)条（关于存放/登记及处理限制）的行为，一经循公诉程序定罪，最高刑罚为罚款20万港元及监禁两年。C错误，罚款100万港元及监禁7年是针对“意图诈骗”而违反相同条款（第5或10(1)条）并经公诉程序定罪时的最高刑罚，而非“无合理辩解”的情况。D错误，严重的“无合理辩解”违规行为本身已可能构成犯罪，将面临刑事检控和包括监禁在内的刑罚，并非仅受纪律处分。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. A fine at level 6 applies to an act of "intent to defraud" violating certain specific provisions (e.g., standing authorization provisions) or is one of the fine options for a violation "without reasonable excuse" upon summary conviction. This question asks about the maximum penalty for a violation "without reasonable excuse" upon conviction on indictment. B is correct. For a violation "without reasonable excuse" of rules 5 or 10(1) of the Client Securities Rules (regarding deposit/registration and handling restrictions), upon conviction on indictment, the maximum penalty is a fine of HK$200,000 and imprisonment for 2 years. C is incorrect. A fine of HK$1 million and imprisonment for 7 years is the maximum penalty for an act of "intent to defraud" violating the same provisions (rules 5 or 10(1)) upon conviction on indictment, not for violations "without reasonable excuse." D is incorrect. A serious violation "without reasonable excuse" may itself constitute a criminal offense, subject to criminal prosecution and penalties including imprisonment, not merely disciplinary action.


### 温习册出处

罚则
罚则取决于中介人或其有联系实体所违反的特定条文。
根据《证券及期货条例》，中介人或其有联系实体违反《客户证券规则》第 4(4)或 12 条，即属犯罪。该两条分别是有关客户常设授权及申报违规事宜的规定。倘意图诈骗而触犯法例的中介人或其有联系实体，一经定罪可处第 6 级罚款。如触犯法例但无合理辩解，则可处第 3 级罚款。
《客户证券规则》第 5 或 10(1)条分别是关于存放或登记客户证券及证券抵押品的规定，以及处理客户证券或证券抵押品的限制，倘中介人或其有联系实体意图诈骗而触犯该两条规则，则最高刑罚为罚款 100 万港元及监禁 7 年（一经循公诉程序定罪），或罚款 50 万港元及监禁 1 年（一经循简易程序定罪）。如触犯法例而无合理辩解，则最高刑罚为罚款 20 万港元及监禁两年（一经循公诉程序定罪），或可处第 6 级罚款及监禁 6 个月（一经循简易程序定罪）。
如属刑事检控，实际刑罚会由法院法官厘定，法官可选择判处违规者监禁。
《客户证券规则》无意处分因行政管理上的错误而并无遵守规则者，所针对的是意图诈骗或无合理辩解理由而违反规则者。
就《客户证券规则》的违规行为，证监会可根据《证券及期货条例》第 194 条采取纪律处分行动。

## 第2题 (ID: 190849)

### 中文题目

客户取消冷静期内的投资。机构从发行人处获得退款（含销售佣金）后，未将佣金部分退给客户，称其为“服务费”。这符合规定吗？
A. 符合，因提供了销售服务
B. 不符合，因销售佣金也必须退还
C. 符合，若客户同意
D. 不符合，因退款不能扣任何费用


### English

A client cancels an investment during the cooling-off period. After receiving the refund (including sales commission) from the issuer, the firm does not refund the commission portion to the client, calling it a “service fee.” Is this compliant?
A. Yes,because sales service was provided
B. No,because the sales commission must also be refunded
C. Yes,if the client consents
D. No,because no fees may be deducted from the refund


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，提供销售服务不能成为在冷静期内保留销售佣金的理由。
B正确，从发行人处收回的退款包括销售佣金，该部分必须退还给客户。
C错误，客户同意不能豁免此项具体的退款责任。
D错误，退款可扣除销售前已披露的合理手续费，但佣金不属此类。


### Explanation

A incorrect, providing sales service does not justify retaining the sales commission during the cooling-off period.
B correct, the refund recovered from the issuer includes the sales commission, which must be refunded to the client.
C incorrect, client consent does not override this specific refund obligation.
D incorrect, the refund may deduct pre-disclosed reasonable handling charges, but the commission is not such a charge.


### 温习册出处

退款责任
倘客户根据投资产品所附设的冷静期机制行使其权力取消买卖盘、向发行人售
回产品或进行平仓交易，则持牌人或注册人应即时执行客户的指示，并向其悉
数退还从产品发行人收回的退款（包括销售佣金），有关退款已扣除销售时或在
销售前向客户披露的合理手续费，且不含任何边际利润。

## 第3题 (ID: 190878)

### 中文题目

一家持牌法团作为受托人处理客户资产。证监会通过订立规则来保障这些资产，其规则主要涵盖以下哪两个具体方面？
A. 公司的资本充足率与高管人员的任职资格。
B. 客户资产的持有、处理以及客户款项的持有、处理。
C. 产品销售时的风险披露与广告宣传的合规性。
D. 交易系统的安全标准与数据备份的频率。


### English

A licensed corporation handles client assets as a trustee. The SFC safeguards these assets by making rules that primarily cover which two specific areas?
A. The company&#039;s capital adequacy and the qualifications of its senior management.
B. The holding and handling of client assets,and the holding and handling of client money.
C. Risk disclosure during product sales and the compliance of advertisements.
D. The security standards of trading systems and the frequency of data backups.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，虽然资本充足率和高管资格是重要的审慎监管要求，但它们属于中介人整体财务状况和治理的范畴，并非专门针对“客户资产”这一特定财产的保障规则的核心内容。B正确，因为保障投资者资产的具体规则，其核心就是规范两类核心客户财产的处理：一是客户资产及证券抵押品的持有与处理，二是客户款项的持有与处理。C错误，风险披露和广告宣传主要规范的是销售过程中的行为和对客户的告知义务，与客户资产被收取后的安全保管和处理的直接关联性较弱。D错误，交易系统安全和数据备份属于运营和技术风险管理措施，旨在确保业务连续性，而非直接针对客户资产保障订立的专项规则。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. While capital adequacy and management qualifications are important prudential requirements, they fall under the scope of the intermediary&#039;s overall financial condition and governance, not the core content of rules specifically designed for the protection of “client assets” as distinct property. B is correct because the specific rules for protecting investors&#039; assets focus precisely on regulating the handling of two core types of client property: one is the holding and handling of client assets and securities collateral, and the other is the holding and handling of client money. C is incorrect. Risk disclosure and advertising mainly regulate conduct during the sales process and disclosure obligations to clients, and are less directly related to the safe custody and handling of client assets after they have been received. D is incorrect. Trading system security and data backup are operational and technology risk management measures aimed at ensuring business continuity, not specialized rules enacted directly for client asset protection.


### 温习册出处

一般规定
证券及期货事务监察委员会（“证监会”）获授权根据《证券及期货条例》制订规则。考生必须了解正确处理客户的资产，并应实施制度以确保中介人遵从《客户证券规则》及《客户款项规则》的重要性，有关规则旨在保障客户的资产。
证券及期货业的中介人以受托人身份处理包括款项在内的客户资产。证监会透过订立有关下列各项的规则以保障投资者的资产：
(a) 持有及处理客户资产及证券抵押品（根据《证券及期货条例》第 148 条）；及
(b) 持有及处理客户款项（根据《证券及期货条例》第 149 条）。
只有以下人士可在香港收取或持有中介人的客户资产：
(a) 中介人；
(b) 中介人的有联系实体；或
(c) 豁除人士（见下文附注）。
附注︰豁除人士指任何认可财务机构、根据财务通融安排可存放客户证券抵押品的其他中介人、经证监会核准的任何公司或非香港公司，及根据证监会规则指明他为可与其维持指定信托帐户或客户帐户的任何人士（见《证券及期货条例》第 164 条）。
然而，须留意《证券及期货条例》第 150 条的规定：
(a) 《证券及期货条例》第 148 及 149 条（或根据该两条条文所订立的任何规则），不得解释为剥夺或影响任何人士就客户资产所享有的合法申索权或留置权；及
(b) 该等申索权或留置（出非复合式单选题）

## 第4题 (ID: 190939)

### 中文题目

为了确保香港证券市场交易环境的公平、有序和高效，香港交易所需要对其交易系统进行持续的监控、维护和升级。此外，交易活动必须严格遵守交易所制定的相关规则。根据香港交易所的组织架构，负责“管理及监察现货及衍生产品交易系统”的营运单位是哪一个？
A. 资讯技术科
B. 营运科
C. 结算科
D. 风险管理科


### English

To ensure the fairness, orderliness, and efficiency of Hong Kong’s securities market trading environment, HKEX needs to conduct continuous monitoring, maintenance, and upgrades of its trading systems. Furthermore, trading activities must strictly comply with the relevant rules established by the exchange. According to HKEX’s organizational structure, which operating unit is responsible for “managing and monitoring the cash and derivatives trading systems”?
A. Information Technology Division
B. Operations Division
C. Clearing Division
D. Risk Management Division


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，资讯技术科可能提供技术支持，但“管理及监察交易系统”作为一项核心交易职能，明确归属于营运科。
B正确，因为营运科的交易职能明确包括“管理及监察现货及衍生产品交易系统”。
C错误，结算科主要负责交易后的结算交收业务，而非交易系统的管理和监察。
D错误，风险管理科主要负责识别和评估风险，而非直接管理交易系统。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The Information Technology Division may provide technical support, but “managing and monitoring trading systems” as a core trading function is explicitly assigned to the Operations Division.
B is correct because the trading functions of the Operations Division explicitly include “managing and monitoring the cash and derivatives trading systems.”
C is incorrect. The Clearing Division is primarily responsible for post-trade clearing and settlement business, not the management and monitoring of trading systems.
D is incorrect. The Risk Management Division is primarily responsible for identifying and assessing risks, not directly managing trading systems.


### 温习册出处

香港交易及结算所有限公司
营运单位
香港交易所的营运划分为 13 科，与本温习手册有关的是营运科和股本证券科。
营运科
营运科负责交易、市场监察和结算及存管的职能。
交易职能
交易职能包括：
(a) 服务及招纳交易所参与者及结构性产品发行商；
(b) 管理现货市场及衍生产品市场交易方面的业务及营运；
(c) 制定二级市场及衍生产品市场的交易规则及政策；
(d) 管理及监察现货及衍生产品交易系统；
(e) 确保交易环境具透明度、公平有序及高效率，并符合由相关交易所设立的有关规则和规定；及
(f) 负责管理沪深港通的业务及营运。

## 第5题 (ID: 190763)

### 中文题目

某平台提供多种虚拟资产交易服务，包括一些非证券型代币。该平台自身也发行代币并担任做市商。投资者张先生在该平台投资后，因平台遭遇黑客攻击而遭受损失，且发现某些代币信息披露不全。根据证监会所列的虚拟资产投资风险，张先生面临的风险可能涉及哪些类别？
I. 网络保安及稳妥保管资产风险
II. 利益冲突风险
III. 欺诈风险
IV. 不同水平的监管保障风险
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. I,II,III,IV


### English

A platform offers various virtual asset trading services, including some non-security tokens. The platform also issues its own tokens and acts as a market maker. Investor Mr. Zhang suffered losses after investing on the platform due to a hacker attack and found incomplete information disclosure for some tokens. According to the SFC&#039;s listed risks of virtual asset investment, which risk categories may Mr. Zhang be exposed to?
I. Cybersecurity and safe custody risks
II. Conflict of interest risks
III. Fraud risks
IV. Different levels of regulatory protection risks
A. I,II,III
B. I,II,IV
C. I,III,IV
D. I,II,III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确。平台遭遇黑客攻击导致投资者损失，属于网络保安及稳妥保管资产风险。
II正确。平台同时发行代币并担任做市商，以多重身份行事可能导致利益冲突。
III正确。某些代币信息披露不全，增加欺诈风险。
IV正确。非证券型代币可能不受招股章程等规定约束，且部分市场失当行为条文可能不适用，属于不同水平的监管保障风险。


### Explanation

I correct. Losses due to a hacker attack fall under cybersecurity and safe custody risks.
II correct. The platform issuing tokens and acting as a market maker may lead to conflicts of interest.
III correct. Incomplete information disclosure increases fraud risks.
IV correct. Non-security tokens may not be subject to prospectus requirements, and some market misconduct provisions may not apply, representing different levels of regulatory protection.


### 温习册出处

投资者风险
证监会已列出以下与投资虚拟资产或虚拟资产期货合约相关的风险︰
(a) 估值、波动性及流通性－虚拟资产典型地缺乏相关实体资产支持或政府担
保，而价格经常会因短期投机活动以及规模较小且较为零散的资金池而受
到影响；
(b) 会计及审计－在会计的专业范畴内并无协订标准，用以确定拥有权或估值
是否合理；
(c) 网络保安及稳妥保管资产－已因网络黑客入侵而损失逾数十亿元；
(d) 市场廉洁稳健－围绕虚拟资产所形成的市场未受监管，当中可能出现违规
的行为手法；
(e) 洗钱及恐怖分子资金筹集风险－由于虚拟资产典型地以不记名方式持有及
买卖，故此存在利用虚拟资产进行洗钱及恐怖分子资金筹集活动的风险；
(f) 利益冲突－提供交易场所的虚拟平台营运者可能以多重身份行事，导致利
益冲突未受规管的情况；
(g) 欺诈－就虚拟资产的披露资料进行的尽职审查可能不足，增加出现欺诈行
为的风险；及
(h) 不同水平的监管保障－不是所有获准在持牌平台买卖的虚拟资产均受相同
水平的法定保障，例如发行并非属证券的虚拟资产无须遵守招股章程规定，
及因为虚拟资产买卖并非在《证券及期货条例》内所界定的“有关认可市场”
（即联交所）进行的缘故，若干《证券及期货条例》的市场失当行为条文并
不适用。

## 第6题 (ID: 190950)

### 中文题目

一家持牌法团计划为一位符合“高资产净值个人”定义的客户提供一项复杂的场外衍生品投资服务。在此情况下，中介人可以依据哪项规则，免除通常向零售投资者提供的部分保障措施？
A. 《证券及期货（杂项）规则》
B. 《证券及期货（专业投资者）规则》
C. 《证券及期货（投资者赔偿－申索）规则》
D. 《证券及期货（发牌及注册）（资料）规则》


### English

A licensed corporation plans to provide a complex over-the-counter derivative investment service to a client who qualifies as a “high net worth individual.” Under these circumstances, which rule allows the intermediary to be exempt from certain protective measures typically provided to retail investors?
A. Securities and Futures (Miscellaneous) Rules
B. Securities and Futures (Professional Investor) Rules
C. Securities and Futures (Investor Compensation – Claims) Rules
D. Securities and Futures (Licensing and Registration)(Information) Rules


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，《证券及期货（杂项）规则》主要涉及向证监会递交文件的一般规定，如展示牌照等，与为专业投资者免除保障措施无关。
B正确，因为该规则明确免除了中介人为专业投资者（包括高资产净值个人）进行交易时某些为保障投资大众而施加的措施。
C错误，该规则涉及的是向投资者赔偿基金提出申索的资格问题，与交易过程中的保障措施豁免无关。
D错误，该规则涉及的是申请牌照和注册时需提交的资料，与针对专业投资者的服务豁免无关。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The Securities and Futures (Miscellaneous) Rules primarily deal with general requirements for documents submitted to the SFC, such as the display of licenses, and are not related to exemptions from protective measures for professional investors.
B is correct. These rules specifically exempt intermediaries from certain measures imposed for the protection of the investing public when dealing for professional investors, which include high net worth individuals.
C is incorrect. These rules pertain to eligibility for making claims against the Investor Compensation Fund and are unrelated to exemptions from protective measures in the course of dealing.
D is incorrect. These rules pertain to information required for license applications and registration and are unrelated to service exemptions for professional investors.


### 温习册出处

《证券及期货条例》第 XVI 部 － 杂项条文

A. 需要，因为国泰资本是执行交易的中介人，对所有通过其执行的交易涉及的资产都有责任。
B. 需要，因为盈富基金是在联交所上市的证券，属于规则覆盖的资产类型。
C. 不需要，因为该证券是客户在并非国泰资本或其关联实体的第三方机构开立的账户内持有的。

### English

Mr. Wong is a client of "Cathay Capital." He opened a securities account directly in his own name at Standard Chartered Bank and holds the Tracker Fund through that account. Cathay Capital only acts as the trade execution broker for this account. In this case, does Cathay Capital have the relevant responsibilities under the Client Securities Rules regarding the Tracker Fund units in Mr. Wong&#039;s Standard Chartered Bank account?
A. Yes,because Cathay Capital is the intermediary executing the trades and is responsible for all assets involved in trades executed through it.
B. Yes,because the Tracker Fund is a security listed on the Stock Exchange,which is an asset type covered by the rules.
C. No,because the securities are held in an account opened by the client with a third-party institution that is not Cathay Capital or its associated entity.
D. No,because securities in a bank account are automatically the responsibility of the bank and are unrelated to the securities intermediary.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，中介人的责任范围与其是否实际持有或控制客户资产相关。仅执行交易指令但未收取或持有相关资产，则不触发《客户证券规则》下的特定保管责任。B错误，虽然盈富基金是适用类型的资产，但规则适用有一个关键前提，即资产必须“由或代表该中介人在香港收取或持有”。本情景中资产由银行持有，不符合此前提。C正确，规则明确排除了对“由中介人的客户以其姓名或名称于该中介人或其有联系实体以外的人士开立的帐户内的客户证券”的适用，此情景与此描述完全一致。D错误，表述过于绝对，银行的责任依据其与客户的协议和相关银行业法规，但这并不自动免除中介人在其他情形下的可能责任；然而在本情景中，不适用《客户证券规则》的直接原因是C所述。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The scope of an intermediary&#039;s responsibility is related to whether it actually holds or controls client assets. Merely executing trade instructions without receiving or holding the relevant assets does not trigger the specific custodial responsibilities under the Client Securities Rules. B is incorrect. Although the Tracker Fund is an applicable type of asset, a key prerequisite for the rules to apply is that the asset must be "received or held in Hong Kong by or on behalf of the intermediary." In this scenario, the assets are held by the bank, which does not meet this prerequisite. C is correct. The rules explicitly exclude from their application "client securities in an account opened by a client of the intermediary in the client&#039;s own name with a person other than the intermediary or its associated entity." This scenario is entirely consistent with that description. D is incorrect. This statement is overly absolute. The bank&#039;s responsibility is based on its agreement with the client and relevant banking regulations, but this does not automatically exempt the intermediary from possible responsibilities in other contexts. However, in this specific scenario, the direct reason the Client Securities Rules do not apply is as stated in C.

(a) 于认可证券市场（即由认可交易所（现仅得香港联合交易所有限公司）运作的证券市场）上市或交易的证券；或属经证监会根据《证券及期货条例》第104 条认可的集体投资计划的权益；及

### 温习册出处

换言之，《证券及期货条例》准许财政司司长透过发出公告，声明某特定金融工具是否被视为期货合约或其他。
进行买卖期货合约（即任何交易所买卖期货合约或根据《证券及期货条例》被视为期货合约的任何金融工具）业务的任何人士必须申领第 2 类牌照或注册。为持牌法团进行期货合约交易的所有员工必须获发牌照为持牌代表，惟其会计师、文员及出纳员除外。为认可财务机构履行期货合约交易之受规管职能的人士须名列于金管局备存的纪录册上，并显示为就该类受规管活动受聘于该认可财务机构的人士。
考生务必了解，“期货合约交易”的释义特别指明交易所买卖合约，而“证券交易”的释义则并无规定“证券交易”须为交易所交易。这意味着任何交易所外（即场外）远期合约及期权的交易均无须被视作为第 2 类受规管活动而申领牌照或注册。然而，倘场外远期合约及期权的相关产品符合“证券”（见《证券及期货条例》）的释义，则须就第 1 类受规管活动而申领牌照或注册。
就衍生工具的事宜而言，香港的发牌架构与部分其他国际司法权区有所不同。在若干情况下，证券牌照包括可换股债券、预托证券、供股发行及由公司发行的认股权证，但不包括期权、备兑及／或非备兑认股权证或远期合约（不论是场内或场外交易）。因此，衍生工具牌照包括期货、远期合约、期权以及备兑和非备兑认股权证（后两者虽然一般在市场上被称为认股权证，但在香港属于期权）。
当中介人设有一分部或部门负责营运各类与衍生工具（包括任何结构性产品）相关的业务，该中介人及其员工可能须就第 1 类及第 2 类受规管活动申领牌照或注册。在以口头或电子方式与客户或准客户进行个人接触的情况下，该行为或造成向他们提供意见，倘该等提供意见的行为完全附带于从事第 1 类及第 2类受规管活动，则有关员工无须就第 4 类及第 5 类受规管活动申领牌照或注册。

Which stage of money laundering does this most likely involve, and what issues exist?

有关市场失当行为的条文载于《证券及期货条例》第 XIII 及 XIV 部，可分为以下三个主要范畴：
(a) 市场失当行为审裁处根据第XIII部成立。第XIII部界定市场失当行为及赋予市场失当行为审裁处权力，以进行涉及市场失当行为的研讯程序；
(b) 第XIV部确立了可在刑事法庭审讯的刑事罪行；及

第XIII及XIV部的条文大致相同（第XIV部略去“疏忽”部分），为同一行为确立了
双轨法律途径。美国、英国及澳洲已采用此成为国际趋势的双轨制度。

所指称干犯的行为性质及证据是否充分，从而决定循民事途径处理还是进行刑
事检控。
证监会将会根据案件的调查结果，决定应循哪一条检举途径。如证监会认为适
宜循民事途径处理案件，在取得律政司司长同意后，则会在市场失当行为审裁
处提起研讯程序，市场失当行为审裁处将就指称干犯的市场失当行为进行研讯
程序所产生的问题或事宜作出裁决。然而，如证监会认为适合循刑事途径处理，
则会将有关实情转告律政司司长，由律政司司长决定应否在法院提出刑事检控。
律政司司长将按照律政司的检控政策，视乎证据是否充分，并基于公众利益而
决定是否检控。根据《证券及期货条例》，证监会亦有权循简易程序
在裁判法院检控较轻的罪行，但律政司司长的检控权力凌驾于证监会之上。

C正确，中介人须就其所代表进行保证金交易的各名客户备存纪录，以显示作出追缴保证金的详情。
D错误，“详情”意味着需要记录包括金额、日期、通知方式等具体信息，而不仅是金额。


### Explanation

A is incorrect. The rules explicitly require that the details of margin calls be recorded.
B is incorrect. An internal log may not be sufficient to meet formal record-keeping requirements. The rules require "keeping records," which implies systematic and verifiable documentation.
C is correct. For each client for whom the intermediary conducts margin trading, records must be kept to show the details of the margin calls made.
D is incorrect. "Details" means that specific information such as the amount, date, and method of notification must be recorded, not just the amount.


### 温习册出处

保证金交易
中介人在订立任何保证金交易（例如从事期货合约的中介人）时，亦须就其所代表进行交易的各名客户备存纪录，以显示：
(a) 存放于该中介人的各类证券抵押品的市值及保证金价值；
(b) 该等证券抵押品的总市值及保证金总值；及
(c) 作出追缴保证金的详情。

## 第38题 (ID: 190595)

### 中文题目

某投资者因市场操纵行为以高价买入股票后，发现价格被人为推高。该投资者试图主张其交易无效并要求撤销交易。根据相关原则，以下哪种说法正确？
I. 交易不会仅因涉及市场失当行为而自动无效。
II. 投资者可主张交易无效并要求退款。
III. 投资者应通过民事索赔追讨损失，而非撤销交易。
IV. 涉及市场失当行为的交易一律视为无效。
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,III
D. III,IV


### English

An investor bought shares at an inflated price due to market manipulation and later discovered the price was artificially raised. The investor attempts to declare the transaction void and seek cancellation. According to relevant principles, which statement is correct?
I. Transactions are not automatically void merely because they involve market misconduct.
II. The investor may declare the transaction void and request a refund.
III. The investor should seek compensation for losses through civil claims rather than voiding the transaction.
IV. Transactions involving market misconduct are always considered void.
A. I,II
B. I,III
C. II,III
D. III,IV


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

I正确。交易不会仅因涉及市场失当行为而自动成为无效或可撤销。
II错误。投资者不能仅以交易涉及市场失当行为为由直接主张交易无效并撤销。
III正确。正确的救济途径是就损失提起民事索赔，而非要求撤销交易本身。
IV错误。涉及市场失当行为的交易并非一律无效，该原则旨在维护市场交易稳定性。


### Explanation

I correct. Transactions are not automatically void or voidable merely because they involve market misconduct.
II incorrect. An investor cannot directly declare the transaction void and seek cancellation solely on the grounds of market misconduct.
III correct. The appropriate remedy is to pursue civil claims for losses, not to void the transaction itself.
IV incorrect. Transactions involving market misconduct are not always void; this principle aims to maintain market transaction stability.

交易不会失效
应注意：交易不得仅因市场失当行为曾就该等交易或因该等交易而发生，而属无效或可使无效。

## 第39题 (ID: 190795)

### 中文题目

某程序化交易系统的预设策略存在漏洞，在市场开盘瞬间产生了一系列极不合理的订单（如报价远偏离市价），这些订单被快速发送至市场，短暂扰乱了正常交易秩序。这主要反映了系统缺乏哪项风控措施？
A. 系统访问日志记录不全
B. 缺乏对系统可能生成并传送干扰市场公平有序运作的指令的监控与防止措施
C. 交易员未及时干预
D. 未使用指定品牌服务器


### English

A flaw in the preset strategy of a program trading system generated a series of highly unreasonable orders (e.g., quotes far deviating from market prices) at the moment the market opened. These orders were rapidly sent to the market, briefly disrupting normal trading order. Which risk control measure does this primarily reflect the system lacks?
A. Incomplete system access logs.
B. Lack of monitoring and preventive measures against the system generating and transmitting orders that may disrupt the fair and orderly operation of the market.
C. Traders failed to intervene promptly.
D. Failure to use a specified brand of servers.


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，日志记录是事后追溯手段，但此处问题的核心在于未能防止有害指令的产生和发出。
B正确，程序买卖系统的风控措施应监控并防止其向市场传送可能是错误或干扰市场公平有序运作的交易指示。系统生成并发送扰乱性订单，表明此项监控与防止措施缺失。
C错误，交易员干预是补救措施，但不能替代系统本身的自动化风险监控功能。
D错误，服务器品牌与系统策略逻辑的风险控制无关。


### Explanation

A incorrect, log recording is a post-event tracing tool, but the core issue here is the failure to prevent the generation and transmission of harmful orders.
B correct, risk management measures for program trading systems should monitor and prevent the transmission of orders that may be erroneous or disruptive to the fair and orderly operation of the market. The system generating and sending disruptive orders indicates this monitoring and prevention measure is missing.
C incorrect, trader intervention is a remedial action but cannot replace the automated risk monitoring functions the system itself should possess.
D incorrect, server brand is unrelated to the risk control of the system&#039;s strategy logic.


### 温习册出处

风险管理
应设有经合理设计的监控措施，以确保：
(a) 其程序买卖系统及买卖程序的稳健性；及
(b) 其程序买卖系统及买卖程序以有利市场廉洁稳健的方式运作，例如监察及防止传送至市场执行可能是错误或干扰市场公平有序的运作的交易指示。

## 第40题 (ID: 190858)

### 中文题目

一家资产管理公司在其公开发布的基金宣传材料中，使用了“本基金过去五年业绩持续超越基准指数”的表述。然而，材料中未同时以显著方式注明“过往业绩并不代表未来表现”的风险提示。这种做法最可能被认为不符合以下哪项要求？
A. 公平及合理的收费
B. 准确的陈述
C. 广告
D. 防止贿赂指引


### English

An asset management company used the statement “this fund&#039;s performance has consistently outperformed the benchmark index over the past five years” in its publicly distributed fund promotional materials. However, the materials did not simultaneously include a prominent risk warning such as “past performance is not indicative of future results.” This practice is most likely considered non-compliant with which requirement?
A. Fair and reasonable charges
B. Accurate representations
C. Advertisements
D. Prevention of bribery guidance


**正确答案：?**


### 答案解析

A错误，因为该表述不涉及费用或收费问题。
B错误，因为“过去五年业绩持续超越基准指数”这一陈述本身如果属实，并不一定构成不准确。问题在于其呈现方式可能产生的误导，这更直接地归属于广告规范。
C正确，因为公开发布的基金宣传材料属于广告范畴。虽然核心陈述可能属实，但省略必要的平衡性风险提示（如“过往业绩不代表未来表现”），可能使广告在整体上具有误导性，违反了广告中不得载有具误导性资料的规定。
D错误，因为题干内容与贿赂无关。


### Explanation

A incorrect, because the statement does not relate to fees or charges.
B incorrect, because the statement “past five years&#039; performance has consistently outperformed the benchmark index,” if true, may not necessarily be inaccurate. The issue lies more with the potentially misleading presentation, which falls more directly under advertisement rules.
C correct, because publicly distributed fund promotional materials constitute advertisements. While the core statement may be true, omitting necessary balancing risk warnings (e.g., “past performance is not indicative of future results”) could render the advertisement misleading as a whole, violating the rule against including misleading information in advertisements.
D incorrect, because the content of the scenario is unrelated to bribery.


### 温习册出处

一般原则1：诚实及公平
一般原则1规定：
“持牌人或注册人在经营其业务时应以诚实、公平和维护客户最佳利益的态度行事及确保市场廉洁稳健”。
就一般原则1而言，《操守准则》规定：
(a)准确的陈述。向客户作出的任何陈述和提供的资料，必须是准确及没有误导成份的。
(b)公平及合理的收费。应公平、合理及诚信地向客户收取费用、差价及收费。
(c)广告。邀请及广告内不得载有虚假、贬抑、具误导成份或有欺骗性的资料。
(d)防止贿赂指引。持牌人或注册人必须熟悉《防止贿赂条例》的规定，并遵从廉政公署所发出的有关指引。
《防止贿赂条例》禁止雇员在未获得雇主许可的情况下索取或收受利益。
《操守准则》一般原则1及其条文构成了适用于所有持牌人或注册人公认的行
为准则的道德规范基准。一般原则1预期持牌人或注册人将其客户的利益及市
场之廉洁稳健置于其个人业务利益之上。为此情况下，持牌人或注册人进行业
务交易时不应作出欺诈行为或采用不公平的手法。
